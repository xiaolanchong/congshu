Intermediate - The Surname Code

(C1040)

A

姓什么？

xìng shénme?

What's your family name?

B

张，弓长张。

Zhāng, gōng cháng Zhāng.

Zhang, the one with the "bow" radical and the character for "long."

A

你妈妈姓什么？

nǐ māma xìng shénme?

What is your mom's surname?

B

吴，口天吴。

Wú, kǒu tiān Wú.

Wu, the one with the "mouth" radical and the character for "sky."

A

进来吧。

jìnlái ba.

Come on in.

A

姓什么？

xìng shénme?

What is your surname?

C

李，木子李。

Lǐ, mù zǐ Lǐ.

Li, the one with the "wood" radical and the character for "son."

A

你妈妈姓什么？

nǐ māma xìng shénme?

What is your mom's family name?

C

陈，耳东陈。

Chén, ěr dōng Chén.

Chen. It's the one with the "ear" radical and the character for "east."

A

姓什么？

xìng shénme?

What's your surname?

D

姜，美女姜。

Jiāng, měinǚ Jiāng.

Jiang, the one with the characters for "beautiful" and "woman."

A

美女姜？

měinǚ Jiāng?

Beautiful woman Jiang?

D

对。

duì.

Right.

A

不对。

bù duì.

Wrong answer.

D

喂，开门啊，开门啊......

wèi, kāimén a, kāimén a......

Hey, open the door! Open the door!
