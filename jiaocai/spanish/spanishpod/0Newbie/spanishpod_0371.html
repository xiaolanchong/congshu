Newbie - Can I have a light (A0371)

A: ¿Tienes fuego?
Do you have a light?
B: Sí, toma mi mechero.
Yeah, take my lighter.
A: Gracias.
Thank you.
B: De nada, pero aquí está prohibido fumar.
You're welcome, but it's forbidden to smoke here.
A: Puff…
Puff...

Key Vocabulary

tener Verb to have
el fuego Noun (m) light
el mechero Noun (m) lighter
prohibido Adjective prohibited
fumar Verb to smoke

Supplementary Vocabulary

la cerilla Noun (f) match
quemarse Verb to burn
perjudicar Verb to damage
la salud Noun (f) health
el cigarro Noun (m) cigarette
