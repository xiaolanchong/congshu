Intermediate - Future Plans (C0066)

李小姐，你大学毕业了吗？
Lǐ Xiǎojie ，nǐ dàxué bìyè le ma ？
Have you graduated from university Miss Li?

我刚毕业。我是从上海大学毕业的。我的专业是商务管理。
wǒ gāng bìyè 。wǒ shì cóng Shànghǎi Dàxué bìyè de 。wǒ de zhuānyè shì shāngwù guǎnlǐ 。
I just graduated. I graduated from Shanghai University. My major is business administration.

好极了。你有什么打算？继续学习还是找工作？
hǎo jíle 。nǐ yǒu shénme dǎsuàn ？jìxù xuéxí hái shì zhǎo gōngzuò ？
Fantastic! What are your plans? Do you want to continue studying or find a job?

我不想继续学习。我打算在一家国际公司工作。
wǒ bù xiǎng jìxù xuéxí 。wǒ dǎsuàn zài yī jiā guójì gōngsī gōngzuò 。
I don’t want to continue studying. My plan is to work in an international company.

这样的打算不错。上海工作的机会很多。
zhèyàngde dǎsuàn bùcuò 。Shànghǎi gōngzuò de jīhuì hěn duō 。
That’s a pretty good plan. There are a lot of job opportunities in Shanghai.
