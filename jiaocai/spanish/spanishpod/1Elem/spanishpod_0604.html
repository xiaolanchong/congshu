Elementary - Love this music (B0604)

A:  ¿Quién toca esta música?
Who plays this music?
B:  Chichi Peralta. Es un cantante muy conocido.
Chichi Peralta. He is a very famous singer.
A:  ¿Es cubano?
Is he Cuban?
B:  No es dominicano. Tengo todos sus discos.
No. He is Dominican. I have all his records.
A:  Voy a bajar su música.
I will download his music.
B:  iTunes tiene la mayoría de sus álbumes, excepto el que toca.
iTunes has the majority of his records, except for the one that is currently playing.
A:  ¡Prestamelo!
Lend it to me!
