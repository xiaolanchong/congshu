Elementary - Scary Movie? (B0129)

A: Il ne fait pas peur ce film d`horreur.
This horror film isn't scary.
B: Oui, je suis déçu...
Yeah, I'm disappointed.
A: Ah, les parents… Ils veulent toujours tout nous interdire…
Our parents always want to forbid us from everything.
B: Ah, c`est juste le chat!
Oh, it's just the cat.
A: Mais, attends, on n`a pas de chat…
But, wait, we don't have a cat..
B: Pourquoi on n`a pas écouté les parents?
Why didn't we listen to our parents?

Key Vocabulary

faire peur Verb to scare
le film d`horreur Noun (m) scary movie
déçu Adjective disappointed
le parent Noun (m) parent
vouloir Verb to want
interdire Verb to forbid
le chat Noun (m) cat
