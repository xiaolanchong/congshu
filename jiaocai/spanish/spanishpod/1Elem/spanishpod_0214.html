Elementary - What a family (B0214)

A: ¿Arturo es tu hermano?
Is Arturo your brother?
B: No, es mi primo.
No, he's my cousin.
A: Entonces Ernesto es tu hermano.
Then Ernesto is your brother.
B: No, es mi tío.
No, he's my uncle.
A: Entonces, ¿quién es tu hermano?
So then, who is your brother?
B: No tengo hermano, tengo hermana.
I don't have a brother, I have a sister.

Key Vocabulary

el hermano Noun (m) brother
el primo Noun (m) cousin
el tío Noun (m) uncle
tener Verb to have
ser Verb to be
quién Pronoun who

Supplementary Vocabulary

la familia Noun (f) family
el sobrino Noun (m) nephew
el hermanastro Noun (m) stepbrother
el padre Noun (m) father
la madre Noun (f) mother
el abuelo Noun (m) grandfather
