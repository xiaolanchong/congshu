Elementary - Cannes Film Festival (B0008)

A: C`est bientôt le Festival de Cannes!
The Cannes Film Festival is coming soon!
B: Je sais, j`y vais chaque année!
I know, I go there every year!
A: Comment tu fais? C`est difficile d`avoir des places!
How do you manage to do that? It's hard to get tickets!
B: Ouais, mais mon père est réalisateur.
Yeah, but my father is a director.
A: T`as de beaux yeux, tu sais!
You've got beautiful eyes, you know that?
