Elementary - Best Friends (B0199)

A:  Encontré esta foto, ¿con quién estás?
I found this picture... who are you with?
B:  Uuuyy, esta foto es viejísima, se llama Sandra.
Wow, this picture is super old; her name is Sandra.
A:  ¿Y quién es?
And who is she?
B:  Era mi mejor amiga de la primaria.
She was my best friend in elementary school.
