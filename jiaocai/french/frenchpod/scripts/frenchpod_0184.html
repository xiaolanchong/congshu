Newbie - It's on me (A0184)

A: Tu veux boire quelque chose? Je t`invite!
Do you want to drink something? It`s on me!
B: Heu... d`accord.
Er... OK.
A: Tiens, je t`ai pris une Margarita!
There you go, I got you a Margarita!
B: Désolée, je ne bois pas d`alcool.
Sorry, I don`t drink alcohol.

Key Vocabulary

vouloir Verb to want
boire Verb to drink
quelque chose Pronoun something
d`accord Phrase OK
prendre Verb to take
l`alcool Noun (m) alcohol

Supplementary Vocabulary

manger Verb to eat
payer Verb to pay
la boisson Noun (f) drink
le jus de fruit Noun (m) fruit juice
le cocktail Noun (m) cocktail
