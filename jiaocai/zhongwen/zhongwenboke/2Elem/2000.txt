{Elementary - No TP} (B2083)
A:    哎，厕所里没有纸。糟了！
   ái, cèsuǒ lǐ méiyǒu zhǐ. zāo le!
  Hey! There's no toilet paper in the bathroom! Dammit!
A:    外面有人吗？请问外面有人吗？
   wàimiàn yǒu rén ma? qǐngwèn wàimiàn yǒu rén ma?
  Is there someone out there? Excuse me, is there someone out there?
B:    你怎么了？
   nǐ zěnmele?
  What is it?
A:    厕所里没有纸。我也没有带纸。
   cèsuǒ lǐ méiyǒu zhǐ. wǒ yě méiyǒu dài zhǐ.
  There's no toilet paper in the bathroom. I didn't bring any tissues.
A:    你能给我点儿吗？
   nǐ néng gěi wǒ diǎnr ma?
  Can you give me some?
B:    你需要纸，是吗？
   nǐ xūyào zhǐ, shì ma?
  You need toilet paper, right?
A:    对对对！
   duì duì duì!
  Yes! Yes! Yes!
B:    我有。我从下面给你！
   wǒ yǒu. wǒ cóng xiàmiàn gěi nǐ!
  I have some. I'll give you some from below.
A:    太谢谢你了！
   tài xièxie nǐ le!
  Thank you so much!
