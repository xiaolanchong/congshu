Newbie - Are you single? (A0086)

A: ¿Eres soltero?
Are you single?
B: Sí, ¿Y tú?
Yes. How about you?
A: No, tengo novio.
No, I have a boyfriend.
B: Qué lástima…
That's too bad...

Key Vocabulary

ser Verb to be
soltero Adjective single
tener Verb to have
novio Noun (m) boyfriend
lástima Noun (f) pity

Supplementary Vocabulary

pareja Noun (f) couple
relación Noun (f) relationship
comprometido Adjective engaged
casado Adjective married
divorciado Adjective divorced
separado Adjective separated
