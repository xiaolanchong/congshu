Newbie - Are you single? (A0086)

A:  ¿Eres soltero?
Are you single?
B:  Sí, ¿Y tú?
Yes. How about you?
A:  No, tengo novio.
No, I have a boyfriend.
B:  Qué lástima…
That's too bad...
