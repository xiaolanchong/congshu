Elementary - Do you have some change? (B0067)

A: Tu as de la monnaie?
Do you have some change?
B: Tiens, j'ai une pièce de deux euros.
Here, I have a two-Euro coin.
A: Il était vraiment bien ce joueur de violon.
That violin player was really good.
B: Oh, regarde. Qu`est-ce que c'est que ça? Il a même un micro.
Oh, look. What the heck is that? He even has a microphone.
A: Oh, non! Il ne sait vraiment pas chanter!
Oh, no! He really does not know how to sing.
B: Il y a encore trois arrêts! Vite, changeons de wagon!
There are still three stops left. Hurry, let's change cars.

Key Vocabulary

la monnaie Noun (f) change
la pièce Noun (f) coin
le joueur Noun (m) player
savoir Verb to know
chanter Verb to sing
encore Adverb again
l`arrêt Noun (m) stop
le wagon Noun (m) car

Supplementary Vocabulary

le musicien Noun (m) musician
l`instrument Noun (m) instrument
le billet Noun (m) note
chanter faux Phrase to sing out of tune
le métro Noun (m) French metro
le mendiant Noun (m) beggar
