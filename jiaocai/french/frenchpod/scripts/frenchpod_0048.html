Newbie - What is this? (A0048)

A: Qu`est-ce que c`est?
What is this?
B: Quoi?
What?
A: Le bouton rouge là… Qu`est-ce que c`est?
The red button there... what is it?
B: Non, ne touche pas! Non…
No, don`t touch it! No...

Key Vocabulary

qu`est-ce que Phrase what
le bouton Noun (m) button
rouge Adjective red
là Adverb right there
toucher Verb to touch

Supplementary Vocabulary

le vert Noun (m) green
le feu Noun (m) traffic light
le danger Noun (m) danger
interdit Adjective forbidden
l`avertissement Noun (m) warning
