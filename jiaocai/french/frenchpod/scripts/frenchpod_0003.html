Newbie - Bad Movie (A0003)

A: Qu`est-ce que tu en penses?
What do you think?
B: Ce film est nul.
This film is terrible.
A: Je suis d`accord.
I agree.
B: On y va?
Shall we go?
A: OK.
OK.

Key Vocabulary

penser Verb to think
le film Noun (m) film
nul Adjective terrible
être Verb to be
d`accord Phrase of accord
