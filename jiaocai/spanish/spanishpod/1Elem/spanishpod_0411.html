Elementary - Fishy (B0411)

A: ¿No crees que Pepito está raro?
Don't you think that Pepito is acting strange?
B: Yo lo veo normal.
Looks normal to me.
A: Pues algo no me cuadra.
Well is not quite right.
B: ¿Sospechas algo?
Do you suspect something?
A: Sí, me huele que aquí hay gato encerrado.
Yes, something smells fishy to me.

Key Vocabulary

creer Verb to believe
raro Adjective strange
cuadrar Verb to balance
sospechar Verb to suspect
haber gato encerrado Phrase something is fishy

Supplementary Vocabulary

ocultar Verb to hide
la conspiración Noun (f) conspiracy
sospechoso Adjective suspicious
el secretismo Noun (m) secrecy
malas intenciones Phrase bad intentions
