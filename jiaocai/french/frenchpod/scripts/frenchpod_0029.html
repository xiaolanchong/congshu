Newbie - I don't know (A0029)

A: Où est le taxi?
Where is the taxi?
B: Je ne sais pas.
I don`t know.
A: On est en retard.
We're late.
B: Ah! Il arrive!
It's coming!

Key Vocabulary

être Verb to be
en retard Phrase late
où Adverb where
savoir Verb to know
arriver Verb to arrive

Supplementary Vocabulary

l’aéroport Noun (m) airport
en avance Phrase early
quand Adverb when
le bus Noun (m) bus
le chauffeur Noun (m) driver
