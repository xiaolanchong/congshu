Newbie - Forget it (A0197)

A: Luis. No encuentro mis calzones.
Luis, I can't find my underwear.
B: Como siempre.
As always.
A: Ah no, aquí están. Olvídalo.
Oh, no, here they are. Forget it.

Key Vocabulary

olvidar Verb to forget
encontrar Verb to find
aquí Adverb here
el calzón Noun (m) underwear
estar Verb to be
siempre Adverb always

Supplementary Vocabulary

buscar Verb to search for
ahí Adverb there
la llave Noun (f) key
el cajón Noun (m) drawer
dónde Pronoun where
aparecer Verb to turn up
