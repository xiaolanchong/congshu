Intermediate - Buying Flowers (C0388)

A: Buenas tardes, quisiera comprar un bonito arreglo floral.
Good afternoon, I would like to buy a pretty floral arrangement.
B: ¿Qué tipo de flor querría?
What kind of flowers would you like?
A: No sé mucho de flores. Quisiera algo de color blanco, amarillo y naranja.
I don't know a lot about flowers. I would like something in white, yellow and orange.
B: Entonces, qué le parece si juntamos unas rosas, unas orquídeas y un ave de paraíso.
Then, what would you think if we put together some roses, orchids and a bird of paradise?
A: Y... ¿algo más barato no tendría?
And... you wouldn't have anything cheaper?
B: Claveles y girasoles.
Carnations and sunflowers.
A: ¡Mucho mejor!
Much better!

Key Vocabulary

comprar Verb to buy
la flor Noun (f) flower
querer Verb to want
juntar Verb to mix
barato Adjective cheap

Supplementary Vocabulary

el ramo Noun (m) bouquet
la planta Noun (f) plant
la floristería Noun (f) flower shop
caro Adjective expensive
florecer Verb to bloom
