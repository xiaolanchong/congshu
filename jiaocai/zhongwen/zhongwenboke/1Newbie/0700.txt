Newbie - Hot Soup (A0796)
A  妈妈，我要喝汤！
  māma, wǒ yào hē tāng! 
  Mommy, I want to eat soup!
B  好的。小心烫！
  hǎo de. xiǎoxīn tàng! 
  OK. Be careful--it's hot!
A  太烫了！
  tài tàng le! 
  It's too hot!
B  小心点儿！
  xiǎoxīn diǎnr! 
  Be more careful!
