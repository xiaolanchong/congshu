Intermediate - Talking to your roommate (C0554)

A:  ¿Pagaste la renta?
Did you pay the rent?
B:  No, mañana.
No, tomorrow.
A:  ¿Tienes un minuto ?, me gustaría hablar contigo.
Do you have a minute? I would like to talk with you.
B:  Sí, dime.
Yes, tell me.
A:  No quiero molestarte, pero llevas más de una semana pagando la renta.
I don't want to nag you, but it has been a week and you haven't payed the rent.
B:  Lo sé, pero necesitaba el dinero y lo usé.
I know, but I needed the money and I used it.
A:  ¿¡Qué!?
What!
B:  Mañana la pago, promesa de honor.
I'll pay it tomorrow, scout's honor.
