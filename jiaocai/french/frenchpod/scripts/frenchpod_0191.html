Elementary - Carnaval (B0191)

A: C`est génial! C`est la fête partout dans la ville!
That`s great. It`s party time everywhere in town.
B: Regarde ces danseurs, j`adore leur costume.
Look at these dancers, I love their outfits.
A: Viens, on va suivre cette troupe de musiciens.
Come on, we`re going to follow this group of musicians.
B: J`ai envie d`aller dans la parade...
I feel like joining the parade.
A: Tu es folle, c`est réservé aux artistes.
You`re crazy, it`s only for the artists.
B: Allez, viens, suis-moi, c`est toi qui as dit que c`était la fête...
Come on, come, follow me, you were the one who said it was party time...

Key Vocabulary

génial Adjective great
partout Adverb everywhere
la ville Noun (f) town
le costume Noun (m) costume
suivre Verb to follow
avoir envie de Verb to feel like
fou Adjective crazy

Supplementary Vocabulary

le déguisement Noun (m) fancy dress
s`amuser Verb to enjoy oneself-to have fun
le masque Noun (m) mask
le confetti Noun (m) confetti
le char Noun (m) float
