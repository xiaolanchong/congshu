Newbie - Meet the family (A0668)

A:  Te presento a mi primo y a mi tío.
Let me introduce you to my cousin and my uncle.
B:  Mucho gusto.
Nice to meet you.
A:  Ella es mi sobrina y allá está mi hermano.
This is my niece and over there is my brother.
B:  ¿Y ella?
And her?
A:  Mi esposa.
My wife.
