Elementary - Going to the pharmacy (B0399)

A:  Necesito este medicamento.
I need this medicine.
B:  ¿Su receta por favor?
Your prescription please.
A:  Este medicamento no lo tenemos.
We don't have this medicine.
B:  ¿Hay alguna otra farmacia cerca?
Is there another pharmacy nearby?
A:  Sí, hay otra a dos cuadras.
Yes, there's another two blocks away.
