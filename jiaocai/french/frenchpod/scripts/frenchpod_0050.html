Newbie - Off to bed! (A0050)

A: Jour!
Day!
A: Nuit!
Night!
A: Jour!
Day!
A: Nuit!
Night!
B: Les enfants! Ne jouez pas avec la lumière!
Children! Don't play with the light!
A: Mais maman…
But mom...
B: Pas de "mais" : c’est l`heure d`aller au lit!
No "but" : it's time to go to bed!

Key Vocabulary

le jour Noun (m) day
la nuit Noun (f) night
l`enfant Noun (m) child
jouer Verb to play
c`est l`heure de Phrase it is time to

Supplementary Vocabulary

dormir Verb to sleep
le parent Noun (m) parent
refuser Verb to refuse
obéir Verb to obey
sage Adjective well-behaved
