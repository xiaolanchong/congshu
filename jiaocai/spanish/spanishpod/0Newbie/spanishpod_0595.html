Newbie - Do you have an Ipod? (A0595)

A:  ¿Qué es esto?
What is this?
B:  Mi reproductor mp3. ¿Tú tienes un Ipod?
My mp3 player. Do you have an iPod?
A:  Si.
Yes.
B:  ¿El pequeño?
The small one?
A:  No ese es el Shuffle. Tengo el Ipod Touch. Con más de 2,000 canciones.
No that is the Shuffle one. I have the iPod Touch. I have more than 2,000 songs.
