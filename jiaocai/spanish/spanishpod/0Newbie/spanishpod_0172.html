Newbie - I'm not going (A0172)

A:  ¿Vas al museo?
Are you going to the museum?
B:  No voy. ¿Tú vas?
I'm not going. Are you going?
A:  Sí, voy ahora.
Yes, I'm going now.
B:  Hasta pronto.
See you soon.
