Newbie - Do you have a light? (A0004)

A: Excusez-moi, vous avez du feu?
Excuse me, do you have a light?
B: Oui, tenez.
Yes, here you go.
A: Heu... et une cigarette aussi?
Er... and a cigarette too?
B: Vous plaisantez!
You must be joking!

Key Vocabulary

vous Pronoun you
avoir Verb to have
le feu Noun (m) fire
aussi Adverb too
plaisanter Verb to be joking

Supplementary Vocabulary

le paquet Noun (m) pack
le briquet Noun (m) lighter
le tabac Noun (m) tobacco shop
fumer Verb to smoke
les allumettes Noun (f) matches
