Newbie - Too late (A0439)

A:  ¿ Compraste los billetes?
Did you buy the tickets?
B:  Aún no, voy hoy.
I haven't yet, I'm going now.
A:  Demasiado tarde.
You're too late.
B:  ¿Por qué?
Why?
A:  Ya están agotados.
They're already sold out.
