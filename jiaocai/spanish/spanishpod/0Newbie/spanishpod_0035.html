Newbie - Is anyone in there? (A0035)

A: ¡Está ocupado!
It's busy!
B: Ah, disculpe.
Oh, sorry.
A: No hay problema.
No problem.
B: Puede pasar.
You can go in.

Key Vocabulary

ocupar Verb to occupy
disculpar Verb to excuse
haber Verb to exist
el problema Noun (m) problem
pasar Verb to pass

Supplementary Vocabulary

disponible Adjective available
el papel higiénico Phrase toilet paper
el jabón Noun (m) soap
lavar Verb to wash
la mano Noun (f) hand
el baño Noun (f) bathroom
