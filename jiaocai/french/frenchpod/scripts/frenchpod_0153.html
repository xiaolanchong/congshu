Elementary - I caught a cold (B0153)

A: Je ne me sens pas bien. J`ai attrapé un rhume.
I don't feel good. I caught a cold.
A: Atchoum!
Achoo!
B: A tes souhaits! Et mets ta main devant ta bouche! Je ne veux pas attraper tes microbes!
Bless you! And put your hand in front of your mouth! I don't want to catch any germs!
A: Pardon. Est-ce que tu peux me passer un mouchoir?
Sorry. Can you pass me a tissue?
B: Tiens. Mais va chez le docteur!
Here. But go to the doctor!
A: Non, je suis déjà allé à la pharmacie et ils m`ont donné des médicaments.
No, I already went to the pharmacy and they gave me some medicine.

Key Vocabulary

se sentir Verb to feel
le mouchoir Noun (m) tissue
attraper Verb to catch
le rhume Noun (m) cold
le médicament Noun (m) medicine

Supplementary Vocabulary

la maladie Noun (f) sickness
la consultation Noun (f) (doctor) visit
soigner Verb to take care of (someone)
le virus Noun (m) virus
l`aspirine Noun (f) aspirin
