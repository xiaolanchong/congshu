Elementary - Something's burning (B0031)

A: Tu sens ça?
Do you smell that?
B: Oui… il y a quelque chose qui brûle, non?
Yes... is something burning?
A: Oh mon Dieu! Il y a le feu! Appelle les pompiers!!
Oh my God! Fire! Call the firemen!
B: D`accord, je compose le 18! Heu... attends, tu n'as pas oublié le gratin dans le four?
OK, I'll dial 18! Hey, wait... did you forget the gratin in the oven?
A: Le quoi? Oh, non, mon gratin dauphinois!
The what? Oh, no! My au gratin potatoes!

Key Vocabulary

sentir Verb to smell
brûler Verb to burn
le feu Noun (m) fire
le pompier Noun (m) firefighter
composer Verb to dial
le gratin Noun (m) gratin
le four Noun (m) oven
