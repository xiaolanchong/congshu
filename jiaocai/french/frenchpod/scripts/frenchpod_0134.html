Elementary - I have a secret (B0134)

A: J`ai un secret à te dire.
I have a secret to tell you.
B: Dis-moi.
Tell me.
A: D`accord. Mais ferme la porte d`abord.
OK. But close the door first.
B: OK, C`est fait. Qu`est-ce que c`est?
OK, it's done. What is it?
A: Je n`ai pas vraiment de secret. Je n`avais pas envie de me lever. C`est tout.
I don't really have a secret. I didn't feel like getting up. That's all.
B: Tu es vraiment paresseux.
You are so lazy.

Key Vocabulary

le secret Noun (m) secret
fermer Verb to shut
la porte Noun (f) door
d`abord Adverb first
l`envie Noun (f) want
se lever Verb to get up
paresseux Adjective lazy

Supplementary Vocabulary
