Newbie - It's open (A0195)

A: Qui est là?
Who`s there?
B: C`est le facteur.
It is the mailman.
A: Entrez, c`est ouvert.
Come in, it`s open.
B: Je vais revenir plus tard...
I`ll come again later...

Key Vocabulary

le facteur Noun (m) mailman
entrer Verb come in
ouvert Adjective open
revenir Verb to come again
plus tard Adverb later

Supplementary Vocabulary

la lettre Noun (f) letter
le chien Noun (m) dog
la porte Noun (f) door
fermé Adjective closed
la maison Noun (f) house
