Newbie - Hide and seek (A0245)

A: On joue à cache-cache?
Shall we play hide and seek?
B: Ca marche, je compte jusqu'à dix.
Ok, I`ll count to ten.
B: Un, deux, trois, quatre, cinq, six, sept, huit, neuf, dix, j' arrive !
One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, I`m coming.
B: Trouvé !
Found you.

Key Vocabulary

ça marche ok
compter to count
arriver to arrive
trouver to find
jusqu'à up to

Supplementary Vocabulary

zéro zero
vingt twenty
cinquante fifty
cent one hundred
mille one thousand
