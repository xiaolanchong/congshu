Newbie - Anybody home? (A0647)

A:  Hola. ¿Hay alguien en casa?
Hello. Is anyone home?
B:  ¿A quién busca?
Who are you looking for?
A:  Al Sr. Alonso.
Mr. Alonso.
B:  No está. Salió. Soy la vecina.
He is not here. He went out. I am his neighbor.
A:  Le dice que vino Mauricio a buscarlo.
Can you tell him that Mauricio came looking for him?
