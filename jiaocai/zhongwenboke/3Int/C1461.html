Intermediate - A Magazine Subscription (C1461)

A:  小刚，发什么呆呢？
Xiǎogāng, fā shénme dāi ne?
Xiao Gang, what are you spacing out about?
B:  女朋友快过生日了，我在想送她什么比较好。
nǚpéngyou kuài guò shēngrì le, wǒ zài xiǎng sòng tā shénme bǐjiào hǎo.
It's almost my girlfriend's birthday. I'm thinking about what would be a good gift to get her.
A:  项链、耳环、香水、化妆品都行啊。
xiàngliàn、 ěrhuán、 xiāngshuǐ、 huàzhuāngpǐn dōu xíng a.
A necklace, earrings, perfume, make up; those are all OK.
B:  每次都是这些，多俗啊！
měicì dōu shì zhèxiē, duō sú a!
I get her those things every time. They're co cliché!
A:  哎，她喜欢看时尚杂志吗？
āi, tā xǐhuan kàn shíshàng zázhì ma?
Hey, does she like reading fashion magazines?
B:  喜欢。老在街上买那个什么……
xǐhuan. lǎo zài jiēshang mǎi nàge shénme……
Yeah. She's always buying those when she's out and about...
A:  那你可以订一年的送给她啊！
nà nǐ kěyǐ dìng yī nián de sònggěi tā a!
Then you can order her a year's subscription as a gift!
B:  不错，好主意！
bùcuò, hǎo zhǔyi!
Not bad! That's a good idea!
A:  订月刊的话，一个月一期，一期大概20块，一年两百多。
dìng yuèkān dehuà, yī ge yuè yī qī, yī qī dàgài èrshí kuài, yī nián liǎng bǎi duō.
If you order it monthly, you'll get one issue per month. One issue is about 20 kuai, so one year is about 200.
B:  就算是半月刊，一年也不到五百块。
jiùsuàn shì bànyuèkān, yī nián yě bù dào wǔbǎi kuài.
Even if it's bi-monthly, one year wouldn't be more than 500 kuai.
A:  就是，又划算又有创意。
jiùshì, yòu huásuàn yòu yǒu chuàngyì.
That's right. Not only is it worth the price, it's creative too.
B:  好，我明天就去邮局订。谢啦，美女。
hǎo, wǒ míngtiān jiù qù yóujú dìng. xiè la, měinǚ.
OK, tomorrow I'll go to the post office and order it. Thanks, sweetheart.
