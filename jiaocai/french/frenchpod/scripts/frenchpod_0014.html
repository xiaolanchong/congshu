Elementary - Interview stress (B0014)

A: Je suis stressée.
I`m stressed out.
B: Pourquoi?
Why?
A: J`ai un entretien demain.
I have an interview tomorrow.
B: Détends-toi, tout va bien se passer.
Relax, everything is going to be alright.
A: Mais c`est en anglais!
But it`s in English!
B: Mince... Comment on dit "bon courage" en anglais?
Gosh... How do you say "good luck with that" in English?

Key Vocabulary

stressé Adjective stressed out
l`entretien Noun (m) interview
se détendre Verb to relax
tout Pronoun everything
se passer Verb to happen
en Preposition in
bon courage Phrase good luck with that

Supplementary Vocabulary

le travail Noun (m) job
détendu Adjective relaxed
nerveux Adjective anxious
la détente Noun (f) relaxation
se calmer Verb to calm down
engager Verb to hire
le chômage Noun (m) unemployment
