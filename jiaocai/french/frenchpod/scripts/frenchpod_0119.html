Elementary - Lost in the Mountains (B0119)

A: C`est la troisième fois qu`on passe devant ce rocher.
This is the third time that we've gone by this rock.
B: Fais-moi confiance.
Trust me.
A: On n`a pas pris le bon chemin.
We didn't take the right way.
B: Mais si, regarde la carte de la montagne, on est ici et maintenant il faut aller par là. Viens!
Yes, we did. Look at the map of the mountain. We`re here and we have to go this way. Come!
A: Attention, c`est dangereux...
Be careful, it's dangerous...
B: Ah...
Ah...

Key Vocabulary

le rocher Noun (m) rock
Fais-moi confiance Phrase trust me
bon Adjective good
si Adverb yes (to insist)
la carte Noun (f) map
attention Interjection watch out
dangereux Adjective dangerous
