Newbie - It's not me! (A0550)

A:  ¡Eugenio!
Eugenio!
B:  ¿Es conmigo?
Are you talking to me?
A:  ¡Ay! Perdón.
Oh! I'm sorry.
B:  No hay problema.
No problem.
A:  ¡Tiene usted un doble!
You have a twin out there!
B:  ¡Interesante!
Interesting!
