Elementary - Bank Hours (B0267)

A:  Voy al banco.
I'm going to the bank.
B:  Ahora está cerrado.
It's closed now.
A:  ¿Qué días está abierto?
What days is it open?
B:  De lunes a viernes de ocho a cuatro.
From Monday to Friday from eight to four.
A:  Voy mañana entonces.
Then I'll go tomorrow.
