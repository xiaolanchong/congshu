Newbie - Can I borrow money? (A0137)

A: ¿Me prestas dinero?
Will you lend me some money?
B: Sí.
Yes.
A: Te lo devuelvo mañana.
I'll pay you back tomorrow.
B: No hay problema.
It's no problem.

Key Vocabulary

prestar Verb to lend
el dinero Noun (m) money
devolver Verb to give back
haber Verb to exist
problema Noun (m) problem

Supplementary Vocabulary

el préstamo Noun (m) loan
el favor Noun (m) favor
la confianza Noun (f) confidence
deber Verb to owe
la deuda Noun (f) debt
