Elementary - The Hunting Trip (B0124)

A: Chut, ne fais pas de bruit.
Sh, don't make any noise.
B: Oh pardon.
Oh, sorry.
A: Là-bas! Les feuilles ont bougé. Je crois qu`il y a un cerf.
Over there! The leaves moved. I think that there's a deer.
B: Chhuuut, n`effraie pas la bête.
Ssshh, don't scare the creature.
A: Attends... Mais ce cerf porte des vêtements.
Wait... but that deer is wearing clothes.
B: Arrêtez le massacre des animaux !!
Stop the killing of animals!

Key Vocabulary

la feuille Noun (f) leaf
bouger Verb to move
le cerf Noun (m) deer
effrayer Verb to frighten
la bête Noun (f) beast
porter Verb to wear
le massacre Noun (m) massacre
l`animal Noun (m) animal

Supplementary Vocabulary
