Newbie - I can't hear you (A0176)

A:  Mañana voy a tu casa.
I'm going to your house tomorrow.
B:  ¿Perdona? No te oigo.
Sorry? I can't hear you.
A:  MAÑANA VOY A TU CASA.
TOMORROW I'M GOING TO YOUR HOUSE.
B:  Ahora sí te oigo. Hasta mañana.
I can hear you now. See you tomorrow.
