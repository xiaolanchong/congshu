Newbie - Sold Out (A0217)

A:  ¿Tiene donas de chocolate?
Do you have chocolate doughnuts?
B:  Están agotadas. Hay glaseadas.
They are sold out. There are glazed.
A:  Deme cinco.
Give me five.
