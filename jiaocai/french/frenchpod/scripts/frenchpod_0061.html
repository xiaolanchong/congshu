Newbie - Goodbye, my love... (A0061)

A: Au revoir ma chérie.
Goodbye, my love.
B: Au revoir.
Goodbye.
A: Vraiment, je dois y aller, Sophie...
Really, I have to go, Sophie...
B: Quoi? Qui est Sophie? Adieu!
What? Who is Sophie? Goodbye forever!

Key Vocabulary

la chérie Noun (f) darling
vraiment Adverb really
aller Verb to go
qui Pronoun who
être Verb is

Supplementary Vocabulary

l'amour Noun (m) love
embrasser Verb to kiss
la gaffe Noun (f) blunder
la rupture Noun (f) break-up
la colère Noun (f) anger
