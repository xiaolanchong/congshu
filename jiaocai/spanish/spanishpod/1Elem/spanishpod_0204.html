Elementary - He fell out of a tree! (B0204)

A:  ¿Qué hace Juan con muletas?
What's Juan doing with crutches?
B:  Se fracturó la pierna.
He broke his leg.
A:  ¿Cómo?
How?
B:  Se cayó de un árbol.
He fell out of a tree.
A:  Por suerte fue la pierna y no la cabeza.
He's lucky he broke his leg, and not his head.
