Newbie - Where are you from? (A0007)

A:  ¿De dónde eres?
Where are you from?
B:  Soy de Perú ¿y tú?
I am from Peru and you?
A:  Yo soy de Colombia.
I am from Colombia.
B:  ¡Qué bien!
That's great!
