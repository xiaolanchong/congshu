Newbie - Are you sick? (A0204)

A: Debout!
Get up!
A: Il est déjà six heures.
It`s already six.
A: Il faut aller travailler.
You`ve got to go to work.
A: Bon, si c`est comme ça...
Ok, if that`s how it`s gonna be...
B: Tu es malade? On est dimanche!
Are you crazy!? It`s Sunday!

Key Vocabulary

debout Interjection get up
déjà Adverb already
l`heure Noun (f) o`clock
aller Verb to go
travailler Verb to work
bon Interjection ok
le dimanche Noun (m) Sunday

Supplementary Vocabulary

le lundi Noun (m) Monday
le lit Noun (m) bed
difficile Adjective difficult
se lever Verb to get up
paresseux Adjective lazy
