Elementary - What a cheapskate! (B0194)

A:  Papá, ¿me das dinero?
Dad, will you give me some money?
B:  ¿Para qué?
For what?
A:  Para ir al cine.
To go to the movies.
B:  Aquí tienes y me traes el cambio.
Here you go, and you're bringing me back the change.
A:  ¡Qué codo!
What a tightwad!
