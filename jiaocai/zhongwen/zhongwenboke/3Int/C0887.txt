Intermediate - Deodorant in China

(C0887)

A

你在喷香水啊？

nǐ zài pēn xiāngshuǐ a?

Are you wearing cologne?

B

不是，是止汗剂。

bù shì, shì zhǐhànjì.

No, its deodorant.

A

你汗很多吗？

nǐ hán hěn duō ma?

Do you sweat a lot?

B

多，不用止汗剂，臭死了。

duō, bùyòng zhǐhànjì, chòu sǐ le.

Yes. If I don't used deodorant, I will stink.

A

这个东西有用吗？

zhè ge dōngxi yǒuyòng ma?

Does it work?

B

还可以，但是有点儿贵。

hái kěyǐ, dànshì yǒudiǎnr guì.

Its not bad, but it is a little pricey.

A

多少钱？

duōshao qián?

How much?

B

这么一小瓶要几十块。

zhème yī xiǎopíng yào jǐshí kuài.

A small bottle is several dollars.

A

啊？

ǎ?

What?

B

不过总比身上臭好。

bùguò zǒng bǐ shēnshàng chòu hǎo.

But it does take care of the odor.

A

嗯，我也去买一瓶。我女朋友老说我有狐臭。

ng4, wǒ yě qù mǎi yī píng. wǒ nǚpéngyou lǎo shuō wǒ yǒu húchòu.

Hmm, Ill have to get a bottle.  My girlfriend is always telling me I have B.O.
