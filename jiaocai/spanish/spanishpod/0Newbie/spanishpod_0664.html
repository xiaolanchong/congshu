Newbie - Do you know him? (A0664)

A: ¿Conoces a ese chico?
Do you know that guy?
B: ¿A cuál?
Which one?
A: El de camisa azul.
The one in the blue shirt.
B: Sí. Se llama José Andrés y es de Mallorca.
Yes. His name is Jose Andres and he is from Mallorca.
A: Preséntamelo.
Introduce me to him.
B: Eres una coqueta.
You are such a flirt.

Key Vocabulary

conocer to know
el chico guy
cuál which
llamarse to be called
coqueta flirt

Supplementary Vocabulary

coquetear to flirt
la cita date
gustar to like
guapo handsome
el amante lover
