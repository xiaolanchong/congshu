Elementary - La Cucaracha Song (B0334)

La cucaracha, la cucaracha,
The cockroach, the cockroach,
ya no puede caminar.
cannot walk anymore.
Porque le falta, porque no tiene,
Because he doesn't have, because he doesn't have,
las dos patitas de atrás.
two hind legs.

Key Vocabulary

la cucaracha Noun (f) cockroach
caminar Verb to walk
faltar Verb to lack
atrás Adverb behind
porque Conjunction because

Supplementary Vocabulary

delante Adverb in front
despacio Adverb slowly
intentar Verb to try
la canción Noun (f) song
cansado Adjective tired
la fuerza Noun (f) energy
