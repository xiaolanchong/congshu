Newbie - Shave it! (A0426)

A: ¿Y esa barba?
What's the deal with the beard?
B: ¿Te gusta?
Do you like it?
A: No, ¡rasúrate!
No, shave it!
B: No tengo rastrillo.
I don't have a razor.
A: ¡Pues compra uno!
Well, buy one!

Key Vocabulary

la barba Noun (f) beard
rasurarse Noun (m) to shave oneself
el rastrillo Noun (m) razor
tener Verb to have
gustar Verb to give pleasure

Supplementary Vocabulary

el bigote Noun (m) moustache
afeitarse Verb to shave
feo Adjective ugly
barba de tres días Phrase stubbly
pinchar Verb to scratch
