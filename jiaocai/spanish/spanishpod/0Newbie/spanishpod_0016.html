Newbie - I feel sick (A0016)

A: Hombre, ¿qué te pasa?
Dude, what's going on with you?
B: Me siento mal.
I'm not feeling well.
A: ¿Qué tienes?
What's wrong?
B: Me duele el estómago.
My stomach hurts
A: Pobrecito.
Poor guy.

Key Vocabulary

el hombre Noun (m) man
qué te pasa Phrase what is going on with you
sentirse Verb to feel
mal Adverb badly
doler Verb to hurt
pobre Adjective poor

Supplementary Vocabulary

sentirse fatal Phrase to feel awful
sentirse fenomenal Phrase to feel great
sentirse bien Phrase to feel well
