Elementary - Get in line (B0329)

A: Ve a comprar los boletos.
You go to buy some tickets.
B: ¡Hay mucha cola!
There's a long line!
A: Pues ni modo. Haz cola.
Too bad. Get in line.
B: Está bien, tú estaciona el carro.
Okay, you park the car.

Key Vocabulary

comprar Verb to buy
el boleto Noun (m) ticket
la cola Noun (f) line
hacer Verb to make
estacionar Verb to park

Supplementary Vocabulary

tener Verb to have
la lotería Noun (f) lottery
gustar Verb to like
parado Adjective stopped
la tarta Noun (f) pie
