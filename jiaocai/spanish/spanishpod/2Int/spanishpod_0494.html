Intermediate - Holidays are over (C0494)

A:   ¡Se terminaron las festividades!
The holidays are over!
B:  Ahora regresar a la realidad y regresar a la oficina.
Now we're going back to reality - and back to the office.
A:  La pasé tan bien esta Navidad que subí 5 kilos.
I had such a great time at Christmas that I gained 5 kilos.
B:  No importa. ¿Valió la pena ?
It doesn't matter. Was it worth it?
A:  Totalmente, son mis mejores kilos.
Totally. They're the best kilos I've ever gained.
B:  Yo la pasé muy bien con la familia paterna.
I had a great time with my dad's side of the family.
