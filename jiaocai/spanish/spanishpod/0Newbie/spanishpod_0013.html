Newbie - Doing the Laundry (A0013)

A: Voy a lavar la ropa. ¿Hay detergente?
I am going to wash clothes. Is there detergent?
B: Sí hay, abajo en la cocina.
Yes, downstairs in the kitchen.
A: ¿Dónde? ¿En qué parte?
Where? In what part?
B: No sé. Búscalo.
I don't know. Look for it.

Key Vocabulary

ir Verb to go
lavar Verb to wash
la ropa Noun (f) clothes
haber Verb to exist
el detergente Noun (m) detergent
abajo Adverb downstairs
la cocina Noun (f) kitchen
dónde Adverb where
saber Verb to know
buscar Verb to look for
