Newbie - Table for Two (A0015)

A:  Mesa para dos, si es tan amable.
Table for two, if you'd be so kind.
B:  ¿Tiene reservación?
Do you have a reservation?
A:  No, no tengo.
No, I don't.
B:  No importa. Síganme, por favor.
It doesn't matter. Follow me, please.
