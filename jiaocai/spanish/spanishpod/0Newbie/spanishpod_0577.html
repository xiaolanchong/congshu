Newbie - Asking for help (A0577)

A:  ¿Me ayudas con la bolsa, por favor?
Can you please help me with the bag?
B:  ¡Claro, cómo pesa!
Sure. It's so heavy!
A:  Son las compras de la semana.
It's this week's groceries.
B:  Parecen las de un mes.
Seems like more than a month's worth.
A:  ¡Lo siento!
Sorry.
