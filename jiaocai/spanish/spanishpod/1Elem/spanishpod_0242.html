Elementary - Formal Dinner 1: Introducing your wife. (B0242)

A: Buenas noches.
Good evening.
B: Buenas noches a todos. Le presento a mi querida esposa Matilde.
Good evening to all. I present to you my dear wife Matilde.
C: Mucho gusto caballero.
A pleasure to meet you, sir.
A: El gusto es mío. Tomen asiento, por favor.
The pleasure is mine. Please be seated.

Key Vocabulary

presentar Verb to introduce
la esposa Noun (f) wife
el gusto Noun (m) pleasure
tomar Verb to take
el asiento Noun (m) seat

Supplementary Vocabulary

sentarse Verb to sit down
conocer Verb to meet
el placer Noun (m) pleasure
el marido Noun (m) husband
el señor Noun (m) mister
