Newbie - The Last Metro (A0038)

A: Hé les amis, à quelle heure est le dernier métro?
Hey guys, when is the last metro?
B: À minuit.
Midnight.
A: Zut, il est minuit et quart! Comment je fais?
Darn, it's quarter past midnight! What do I do?
B: Reste avec nous!
Stay with us!

Key Vocabulary

l`ami Noun (m) friend
dernier Adjective last
minuit Adverb midnight
le quart Noun (m) quarter
rester Verb to stay

Supplementary Vocabulary

le métro Noun (m) metro
le train Noun (m) train
le bus Noun (m) bus
midi Adverb noon
partir Verb to leave
