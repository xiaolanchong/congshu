Newbie - Where are you going to eat? (A0146)

A:  ¿Dónde vas a comer?
Where are you going to eat?
B:  Voy a ir al restaurante Casa Paco. ¿Y tú?
I'm going to the Casa Paco restaurant. How about you?
A:  No lo sé.
I don't know.
B:  Pues vente conmigo.
Well, come with me.
A:  Vale.
Alright.
