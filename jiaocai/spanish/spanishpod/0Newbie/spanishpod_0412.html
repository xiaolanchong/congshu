Newbie - Favorite Book (A0412)

A: ¿Qué lees?
What are you reading?
B: Mi libro favorito.
My favorite book.
A: ¿Es una novela?
Is it a novel.
B: No, es una biografía.
No, it's a biography.

Key Vocabulary

leer Verb to read
el libro Noun (m) book
la novela Noun (f) novel
ser Verb to be
favorito Adjective favorite

Supplementary Vocabulary

gustar Verb to like
la ficción Noun (f) fiction
el escritor Noun (m) writer
el amor Noun (m) love
prestar Verb to lend
