Newbie - For what? (A0187)

A: Acércate y cierra los ojos.
Come here and close your eyes.
B: ¿Para qué?
What for?
A: Para ésto...
For this...
B: SMACK.
SMACK.

Key Vocabulary

acercarse Verb to come closer
cerrar Verb to close
el ojo Noun (m) eye
para Preposition for
qué Pronoun what
ésto Pronoun this

Supplementary Vocabulary

abrir Verb to open
besar Verb to kiss
el beso Noun (m) kiss
la sorpresa Noun (f) surprise
ver Verb to see
