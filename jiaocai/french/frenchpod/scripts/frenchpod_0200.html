Newbie - Too windy (A0200)

A: Il y a trop de vent.
There`s too much wind.
B: Accroche-toi!
Hang on!
A: Je vais m `envoler.
I`m going to fly away...
B: Accroche-toi! Trop tard!
Hold on tight! Too late!

Key Vocabulary

il y a Phrase there is
trop Adverb too much
le vent Noun (m) wind
s`accrocher Verb to hang on
tard Adverb late

Supplementary Vocabulary

la neige Noun (f) snow
le soleil Noun (m) sun
pleuvoir Verb to rain
le nuage Noun (m) cloud
le temps Noun (m) weather
