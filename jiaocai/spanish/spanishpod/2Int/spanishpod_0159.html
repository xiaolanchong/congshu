Intermediate - Have you seen the boss? (C0159)

A:  Estiii.
Esti...
B:  ¿Quééé?
Whaaat!?
A:  ¿Has visto al jefe hoy?
Have you seen the boss today?
B:  No, estará en la sala de juntas.
No, he must be in the meeting room.
A:  Ya lo busqué allá, pero no está.
I already looked, but he wasn't there.
B:  Entonces no tengo ni idea. Pregúntale a Lili, igual ella lo sabe.
Then I have no idea. Ask Lili, maybe she knows.
A:  Ya se lo pregunté. Y ella tampoco lo sabe. Igual, todavía no ha llegado.
I already asked her. She doesn't know either. Maybe he hasn't shown up yet.
B:  Es posible porque ayer estaba fatal.
It could be, because yesterday he looked terrible.
