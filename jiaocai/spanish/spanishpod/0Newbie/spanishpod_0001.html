Newbie - Are you ready yet? (A0001)

A: ¿Ya estás lista?
Are you ready yet?
B: Espera un momento.
Wait a moment.
A: Tranquila, todavía tenemos tiempo.
Relax, we still have some time.
B: Menos mal.
Good.

Key Vocabulary

ya Adverb yet
estar listo Phrase to be ready
esperar Verb to wait
momento Noun (m) moment
tranquilo Adjective relaxed
todavía Adverb still
tener Verb to have
tiempo Noun (m) time

Supplementary Vocabulary

estar preparado Phrase to be ready
un momentito Phrase just a second
bueno Adjective good
