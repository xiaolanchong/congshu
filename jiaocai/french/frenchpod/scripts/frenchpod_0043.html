Newbie - It's not too serious (A0043)

A: Aie aïe aïe…
Ouch, ouch, ow....
B: Est-ce que ça va?
Are you OK?
A: J`ai mal !
I'm in pain!
B: Ce n`est pas trop grave…
It's not too bad....
A: Ça fait vraiment mal!
That hurts really bad!

Key Vocabulary

le mal Noun (m) harm
trop Adverb too much
grave Adjective serious
vraiment Adverb really
faire mal Verb to hurt

Supplementary Vocabulary

la douleur Noun (f) pain
la blessure Noun (f) injury
se blesser Verb to hurt oneself
soigner Verb to cure
le pansement Noun (m) adhesive bandage
