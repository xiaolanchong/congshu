Elementary - I hate this shirt! (B0314)

A: No me gusta esta camisa.
I don't like this shirt.
B: Quítatela.
Get rid of it.
A: Es la única limpia.
It's the only clean one.
B: Pues lava tu ropa.
Well, clean your clothes.
A: Cómo odio esta camisa.
I hate this shirt so much.
B: AHHHH….LISTO.
Ahhhh... READY.

Key Vocabulary

la camisa Noun (f) shirt
limpio Adjective clean
lavar Verb to wash
odiar Verb to hate
gustar Verb to like

Supplementary Vocabulary

sucio Adjective dirty
la lavadora Noun (f) washing machine
a mano Phrase by hand
la colada Noun (f) laundry
la falda Noun (f) skirt
