Newbie - You are so beautiful (A0091)

A: Comme tu es belle.
You are so beautiful.
B: Pardon.
Excuse me.
A: Ce n`est pas grave.
It's OK.
B: C'est vraiment gênant.
That's really embarrassing.
A: L`addition, s`il vous plait.
Check, please.

Key Vocabulary

comme Adverb like
belle Adjective beautiful
être Verb to be
gênant Adjective embarrassing
l`addition Noun (f) bill

Supplementary Vocabulary

la politesse Noun (f) politeness
les manières Noun (f) manners
s`excuser Verb to apologize
la honte Noun (f) shame
le rendez-vous Noun (m) date
