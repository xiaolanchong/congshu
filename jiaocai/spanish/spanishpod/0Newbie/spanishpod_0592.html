Newbie - We got robbed! (A0592)

A:  ¡Lucía! ven rápido.
Lucia. Come quick!
B:  ¿Qué son esos gritos?
Why are you shouting?
A:  Nos robaron.
We got robbed.
B:  ¿Qué se llevaron?
What did they take?
A:  La televisión y la computadora.
The television and the computer.
