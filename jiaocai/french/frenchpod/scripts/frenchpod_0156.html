Elementary - The Festival of Lights (B0156)

A: Oh là là, tu n`as pas menti! C`est merveilleux.
Oh my gosh, you weren't kidding. It's wonderful!
B: Ca te plait alors?
So you like it?
A: J`adore. Toutes ces lumières... C`est vraiment splendide.
I love it. All these lights...it's truly stunning.
B: Nous sommes très fiers de cette fête.
We're very proud of this festival.
A: Avec la musique en plus… quelle ambiance!
And with the music, too...what an atmosphere.
B: Aujourd’hui, c`est Lyon la ville lumière.
Today, Lyon is the city of lights.

Key Vocabulary

mentir Verb to lie
la lumière Noun (f) light
l`ambiance Noun (f) atmosphere
splendide Adjective stunning
fier Adjective proud
merveilleux Adjective wonderful
plaire Verb to please
en plus Phrase besides
