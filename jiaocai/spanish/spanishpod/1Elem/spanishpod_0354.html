Elementary - What did you do last night? (B0354)

A:  ¿Qué hiciste anoche?
What did you to last night?
B:  Fui al teatro con Fabiola.
I went to the theater with Fabiola.
A:  ¿Y después qué hiciste?
And what did you do afterward?
B:  ¿Me estás interrogando?
Are you interrogating me?
A:  No, sólo que ayer me dejaste plantada.
No, just that yesterday you ditched me.
