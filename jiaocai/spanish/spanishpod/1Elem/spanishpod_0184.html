Elementary - She speaks little Spanish (B0184)

A:  Ésta es mi amiga, Xiao Wu.
This is my friend, Xiao Wu.
B:  Xiao Wu, por fin nos conocemos. Jorge no para de hablarme de ti.
Xiao Wu, we finally meet. Jorge doesn't stop talking about you.
A:  Xiao Wu habla muy poco español. Háblale en chino.
Xiao Wu speaks very little Spanish. Talk to her in Chinese.
B:  Ah, bueno. Xiao Wu, ¡"nihao"！
Oh, ok. Xiao Wu, nihao!
A:  ¡"Nihao"! ¡Mucho gusto!
Nihao! Good to meet you!
