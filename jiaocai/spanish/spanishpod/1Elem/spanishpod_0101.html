Elementary - Remember Sofía? Episode 2 (B0101)

A: ¿Te acuerdas de Sofía?
Do you remember Sofía?
B: Sí, la muerta. ¿Qué le pasó?
Yes, the dead one? What happened to her?
A: Su cuerpo no aparece.
Her body hasn't turned up.
B: Tal vez no está muerta…
Maybe she's not dead...
A: ¡Qué misterio!
What a mystery!

Key Vocabulary

acordarse Verb to remember
muerto Adjective dead
pasar Verb to happen
cuerpo Noun (m) body
aparecer Verb to appear
misterio Noun (m) mystery
tal vez Adverb maybe
