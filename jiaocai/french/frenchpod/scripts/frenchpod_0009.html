Newbie - Red or White? (A0009)

A: Tu veux du vin?
Do you want some wine?
B: Oui, rouge ou blanc?
Yes, red or white?
A: On mange du poisson...
We`re eating fish...
B: Alors blanc!
White then!

Key Vocabulary

vouloir Verb to want
le vin Noun (m) wine
ou Conjunction or
manger Verb to eat
alors Adverb then

Supplementary Vocabulary

le rosé Noun (m) rosé
boire Verb to drink
le verre Noun (m) glass
le repas Noun (m) meal
la bouteille Noun (f) bottle
