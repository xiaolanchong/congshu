Newbie - What's the address? (A0132)

A: Quelle est l`adresse?
What's the address?
B: 55, rue du faubourg Saint-Honoré
55 Faubourg Saint-Honoré street.
A: Mais, c`est l`adresse de l`Elysée, ça.
But, that's the address of the Elysée Palace.
B: Et alors? Mme Sarkozy aussi aime la pizza.
And so? Mrs. Sarkozy likes pizza, too.

Key Vocabulary

quel Pronoun which
l`adresse Noun (f) address
la rue Noun (f) street
ça Pronoun that
aimer Verb to like

Supplementary Vocabulary

commander Verb to order
la pizzeria Noun (f) pizzeria
l`avenue Noun (f) avenue
l`endroit Noun (m) place
le palais Noun (m) palace
