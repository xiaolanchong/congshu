Elementary - Stop Eating (B0262)

A: ¡Deja de comer!
Stop eating!
B: Casi nunca como papas fritas.
I almost never eat potato chips.
A: Ayer comiste una bolsa.
Yesterday, you ate a bag.
B: ¡Eran chicharrones!
Those were pork rinds!
A: Es lo mismo, la comida chatarra no es saludable. ¡Dame eso!
Same thing, junk food is not healthy. Give me that!

Key Vocabulary

dejar Verb to stop
comer Verb to eat
el chicharrón Noun (m) pork rind
la papa Noun (f) potato
chatarra Adjective junk
saludable Adjective healthy

Supplementary Vocabulary

parar Verb to stop
la comida basura Phrase junk food
tragar Verb to swallow
tragón Adjective big eater
la gula Noun (f) greed
