Elementary - How many in your party? (B0224)

A:  Bienvenidos. ¿Cuántas personas son?
Welcome. How many in your party?
B:  Somos seis.
There are six of us.
A:  ¿Sección de fumadores o de no fumadores?
Smoking section or non-smoking?
B:  De no fumadores, por favor.
Non-smoking, please.
A:  Síganme.
Follow me.
