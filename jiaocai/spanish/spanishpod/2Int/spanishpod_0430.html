Intermediate - Party Crashers(0430)

A:  Ya viste, vinieron Mauricio y Oscar, ¿ quién los invitó?
Did you see that Mauricio and Oscar came, who invited them?
B:  Yo no, creo que se colaron en la fiesta.
I didn't, I think they came uninvited to the party.
A:  Qué inoportunos, espero que Verónica no los vea.
How inconsiderate, I hope Veronica doesn't see them.
B:  Pero, ¿ qué importa que los vea?
But what does it matter if she sees them?
A:  Cómo crees, terminó con Mauricio y con Octavio está peleada.
Come on, she broke up with Mauricio and she fought with Octavio.
B:  Ni modo, no podemos correrlos.
Anyway, we can't make them leave.
A:  Vienen para acá, ignóralos.
Here they come, ignore them.
