Newbie - How's it going? (A0006)

A: Hola, guapa, ¿cómo te va?
Hi, beautiful, how's it going for you?
B: Bien, bien. Y tú, ¿qué tal?
Good, good. What about you? What's up?
A: Todo bien, gracias.
Everything's fine, thank you!
B: Me alegra...
That makes me happy!

Key Vocabulary

guapo Adjective beautiful
bien Adverb good
¿qué tal? Phrase what's up?
todo Pronoun all
gracias Noun (f) thanks

Supplementary Vocabulary

lindo Adjective pretty
bello Adjective beautiful
estar contento Phrase to be happy
mal Adverb badly
normal Adverb normal
regular Adverb so so
fenomenal Adverb great
fatal Adverb really badly
