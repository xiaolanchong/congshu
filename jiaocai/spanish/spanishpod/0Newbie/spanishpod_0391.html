Newbie - Minibar (A0391)

A:  Dame una botella de agua.
Give me a bottle of water.
B:  No hay.
There isn't any.
A:  Sí, en el minibar.
Yeah there is, in the minibar.
B:  Es carísimo, quédate con sed.
It's so expensive, just stay thirsty.
