Elementary - Tell me what you know (B0339)

A: Venga cuéntame lo que pasó.
Come tell me what happened.
B: No sé lo que pasó.
I don't know what happened.
A: Sí que lo sabes.
Yes you know.
B: De verdad que no lo sé.
Honestly I don't know.
A: Anda, venga no te hagas de rogar.
Come on, don't make me beg you.

Key Vocabulary

saber Verb to know
rogar Verb to beg
la verdad Noun (f) truth
venga Interjection come on
contar Verb to tell

Supplementary Vocabulary

todo Pronoun everything
la mentira Noun (f) lie
decir Verb to say
quién Pronoun who
el secreto Noun (m) secret
