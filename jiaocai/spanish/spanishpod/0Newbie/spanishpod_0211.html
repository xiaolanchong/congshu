Newbie - Which bus? (A0211)

A:  ¿Qué autobús va a Medellín?
Which bus goes to Medellín?
B:  El número 503.
The number 503.
A:  ¿A qué hora sale?
What time does it leave?
B:  A las tres.
Three o'clock.
