Elementary - Hiccup (B0396)

A:  ¿Tienes hipo?
Do you have hiccups?
B:  No, si te parece es un resfriado.
No, maybe you think I have a cold.
A:  Qué sarcástico eres.
You're so sarcastic.
B:  Eso por preguntar algo obvio. Ay, no se me quita con nada.
That's what you get for asking something so obvious. Nothing gets rid of this!
A:  ¡Toma agua al revés!
Drink some water in reverse!
