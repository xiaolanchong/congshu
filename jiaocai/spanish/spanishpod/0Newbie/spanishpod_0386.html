Newbie - Tell me the truth (A0386)

A:  Dime la verdad.
Tell me the truth.
B:  ¿Qué quieres saber?
What do you want to know?
A:  ¿La amas?
Do you love her?
B:  No.
No.
A:  ¡Mentiroso!
Liar!
