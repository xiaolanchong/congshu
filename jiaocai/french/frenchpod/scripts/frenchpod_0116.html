Newbie - When's the wedding? (A0116)

A: C`est quand le mariage?
When is the wedding?
B: C`est demain, le vingt-cinq.
It's tomorrow, the twenty-fifth.
A: Mais on est le vingt-cinq aujourd`hui.
But it's the twenty-fifth today.
B: Arrête... Oh non!
Stop...oh, no!

Key Vocabulary

quand Adverb when
le mariage Noun (m) wedding
être Verb to be
demain Adverb tomorrow
aujourd`hui Adverb today

Supplementary Vocabulary

hier Adverb yesterday
avant-hier Adverb the day before yesterday
après-demain Adverb the day after tomorrow
confondre Verb to mix up
le marié Noun (m) groom
