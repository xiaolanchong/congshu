Intermediate - Ways to Break Up

(C2329)

A:

你们说怎么分手不会伤害对方呢？

nǐmen shuō zěnme fēnshǒu bùhuì shānghài duìfāng ne?

How would you all break up with somebody without hurting them?

B:

分手都会伤害对方。

fēnshǒu dōu huì shānghài duìfāng.

Breaking up always hurts the other person.

A:

可是我真的觉得我跟王鹏不合适。

kěshì wǒ zhēnde juéde wǒ gēn Wáng Péng bù héshì.

But I really think that Wang Peng and I aren't good for each other.

C:

很简单，什么都不要说，一个月不联系，不接电话，不回短信。

hěn jiǎndān, shénme dōu bù yào shuō, yī gè yuè bù liánxì, bù jiē diànhuà, bù huí duǎnxìn.

It's easy. You don't need to say anything. Don't communicate for a month. Don't answer the phone. Don't return texts.

B:

那是你们男人的方式。

nà shì nǐmen nánrén de fāngshì.

That's the way you guys do it.

A:

就是，至少要跟对方说清楚吧。

jiùshì, zhìshǎo yào gēn duìfāng shuō qīngchu ba.

Exactly. At least explain it to the other person.

B:

如果说不出口，那就写邮件或者发短信吧。

rúguǒ shuōbuchūkǒu, nà jiù xiě yóujiàn huòzhě fā duǎnxìn ba.

If you can't say it, then just write a letter or a text message.

A:

我试过，他以为我不开心，随便说说的。

wǒ shì guo, tā yǐwéi wǒ bù kāixīn, suíbiàn shuō shuo de.

I tried. He thinks I'm unhappy and just saying whatever comes to mind.

B:

那删掉他所有的联系方式。手机号码和QQ都拉黑。

nà shāndiào tā suǒyǒu de liánxì fāngshì. shǒujī hàomǎ hé QQ dōu lāhēi.

Then delete all of his contact information. Put his phone number and his QQ on a black list.

A:

可我怕他去我家或者来公司找我。

kě wǒ pà tā qù wǒjiā huòzhě lái gōngsī zhǎo wǒ.

But I'm afraid he'll go to my house or come to my work to look for me.

C:

我还有一个办法。

wǒ hái yǒu yī gè bànfǎ.

I have another way.

A:

什么？快说！

shénme? kuài shuō!

What? Tell me!

C:

找他借钱啊！今天借五千，明天借一万，但是不跟他约会。

zhǎo tā jièqián a! jīntiān jiè wǔ qiān, míngtiān jiè yī wàn, dànshì bù gēn tā yuēhuì.

Ask him to lend you some money! Borrow 5,000 today and 10,000 tomorrow, but don't show up on dates with him.

B:

张博你好狠啊！

Zhāng Bó nǐ hǎo hěn a!

Zhang Bo, you're so cruel!
