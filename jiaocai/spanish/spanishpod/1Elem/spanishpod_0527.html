Elementary - Happy Hour (B0527)

A:  ¿Vas a la hora feliz después del trabajo?
Are you going to the happy hour after work?
B:  ¿Dónde?
Where?
A:  En el bar de la esquina. Hay dos por uno.
At the bar in the corner. There are two for one.
B:  ¿Van todos los de la oficina?
Is everyone from the office going?
A:  Si. Ya es bebiernes.
Yes. It's drink Friday.
B:  ¡Ahí nos vemos!
I'll see you there!
