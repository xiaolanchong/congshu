Intermediate - Regional Accents (C0859)

你打算什么时候生小孩？
nǐ dǎsuàn shénme shíhou shēng xiǎohái?
When do you plan to have a child?
来了多少人？
lái le duōshao rén?
How many people came?
我和哥哥都喜欢画画。
wǒ hé gēge dōu xǐhuan huàhuà.
My brother and I like to paint.
