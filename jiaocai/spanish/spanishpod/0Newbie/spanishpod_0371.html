Newbie - Can I have a light (A0371)

A:  ¿Tienes fuego?
Do you have a light?
B:  Sí, toma mi mechero.
Yeah, take my lighter.
A:  Gracias.
Thank you.
B:  De nada, pero aquí está prohibido fumar.
You're welcome, but it's forbidden to smoke here.
A:  Puff…
Puff...
