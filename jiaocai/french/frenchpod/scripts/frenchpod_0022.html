Newbie - Hi, my name is... (A0022)

A: Enchanté, je m'appelle Antoine.
Nice to meet you, my name is Antoine.
B: Enchantée...
It's nice to meet you.
A: Et toi? Comment tu t’appelles?
And you? What's your name?
B: Je m'appelle Sandra.
I am Sandra.

Key Vocabulary

je Pronoun I
s`appeler Verb to be named
enchanté Adjective nice to meet you
comment Pronoun how
tu Pronoun you

Supplementary Vocabulary

bonjour Interjection hello
bienvenue Interjection welcome
le nom Noun (m) surname
le prénom Noun (m) first name
au revoir Interjection bye
