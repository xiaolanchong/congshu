Newbie - Sold Out (A0217)

A: ¿Tiene donas de chocolate?
Do you have chocolate doughnuts?
B: Están agotadas. Hay glaseadas.
They are sold out. There are glazed.
A: Deme cinco.
Give me five.

Key Vocabulary

agotar Verb to sell out
dar Verb to give
la dona Noun (f) doughnut
glasear Verb to glaze
haber Verb to exist

Supplementary Vocabulary

relleno Noun (m) stuffing
querer Verb to want
más Adverb more
otro Adjective another
la rosquilla Noun (f) doughnut
