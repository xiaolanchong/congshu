Newbie - Can I borrow money? (A0137)

A:  ¿Me prestas dinero?
Will you lend me some money?
B:  Sí.
Yes.
A:  Te lo devuelvo mañana.
I'll pay you back tomorrow.
B:  No hay problema.
It's no problem.
