Newbie - At the Bar (A0003)

A: Buenas tardes ¿Qué le sirvo?
Good afternoon, what can I get you?
B: Quiero una cerveza.
I would like a beer.
A: ¿Clara u obscura?
Regular or dark?
B: Obscura por favor.
Dark, please.

Key Vocabulary

buenas tardes Phrase good afternoon
servir Verb to serve
querer Verb to want
cerveza Noun (f) beer
claro Adjective light
obscuro Adjective dark

Supplementary Vocabulary

refresco Noun soda
jugo de naranja Phrase orange juice
agua de jamaica Phrase hibiscus drink
limonada Noun lemonade
agua mineral Phrase mineral water
