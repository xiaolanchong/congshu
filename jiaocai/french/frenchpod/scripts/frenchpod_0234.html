Newbie - Are you sure? (A0234)

A: C' est ici, les vestiaires? Tu es sûr?
Is it here, the locker room? Are you sure?
B: Non, mais je crois.
No, but I think so.
A: Attends, j'ouvre la porte.
Wait, I'm opening the door.
B: Désolé.
Sorry.
