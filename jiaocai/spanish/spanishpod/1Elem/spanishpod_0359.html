Elementary - You snore (B0359)

A: ¿Cómo dormiste anoche?
How did you sleep last night?
B: Pues mal.
Not well actually.
A: ¿De verdad? ¿Por qué?
Really? Why?
B: Porque roncas.
Because you snore.
A: Yo no ronco, o al menos no oigo ronquidos.
I don't snore, or at least I don't hear snores.
B: Un día te voy a grabar para demostrártelo.
One day I'm going to record it and show it to you.

Key Vocabulary

roncar Verb to snore
dormir Verb to sleep
mal Adverb bad
el ronquido Noun (m) snore
oír Verb to hear

Supplementary Vocabulary

bien Adverb well
desvelar Verb to keep awake
el ruido Noun (m) noise
molesto Adjective annoying
el silencio Noun (m) silence
