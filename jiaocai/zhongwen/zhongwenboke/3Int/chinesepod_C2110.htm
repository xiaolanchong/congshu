Intermediate - Upgrading Software (C2110)

A: 啊！小孙，我的防毒软件要到期了，怎么办？
ā! Xiǎosūn, wǒ de fángdú ruǎnjiàn yào dàoqī le, zěnme bàn?
Oh! Xiao Sun, my antivirus software is about to expire. What should I do?
B: 那你还要用吗？
nà nǐ hái yào yòng ma?
Well, do you still want to use it?
A: 这个不错，运行很快。
zhè gè bùcuò, yùnxíng hěn kuài.
It's pretty good. It runs fast.
B: 那你就续费吧。点这里。
nà nǐ jiù xùfèi ba. diǎn zhèlǐ.
Then you should continue it. Click here.
A: 好了。哎，怎么出来了“升级”？
hǎo le. āi, zěnme chūlai le" shēngjí”?
Okay. Hey, why did “upgrade” come up?
B: 哦，这个是问你要不要给软件升级。
ò, zhè gè shì wèn nǐ yào bu yào gěi ruǎnjiàn shēngjí.
Oh, this is asking you if you want to upgrade the software.
A: 我看看。一年199，升级产品两年299。那还是升级吧。
wǒ kàn kan. yī nián yībǎi jiǔshí jiǔ, shēngjí chǎnpǐn liǎng nián èrbǎi jiǔshí jiǔ. nà háishì shēngjí ba.
I see. One year for 199, upgrade the product for two years for 299. Then I'll go ahead and upgrade.
B: 嘿，这就是骗骗你这种人。
hēi, zhè jiùshì piàn pian nǐ zhè zhǒng rén.
Yeah, this is just trying to rip off someone like you.
A: 啊呀，升级合算嘛。
āyā, shēngjí hésuàn ma.
Come on, upgrading is worthwhile!
B: 点“下一步”，去付钱吧。
diǎn" xià yī bù”, qù fùqián ba.
Click “Next Step” and go pay.
A: 哎，信用卡和支付宝都可以用，那我用支付宝吧。
āi, xìnyòngkǎ hé Zhīfùbǎo dōu kěyǐ yòng, nà wǒ yòng Zhīfùbǎo ba.
So, credit cards and Alipay can both be used. Then I'll use Alipay.
