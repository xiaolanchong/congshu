Newbie - How much is it? (A0256)

A:  ¿Cuánto cuesta?
How much does it cost?
B:  Cien euros.
One hundred euros.
A:  ¡Qué caro!
How expensive!
B:  Pues venga en rebajas.
Then come back during the sales.
