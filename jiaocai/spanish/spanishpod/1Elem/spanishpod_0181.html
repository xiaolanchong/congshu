Elementary - Winter is my favorite (B0181)

A: Ya viene el frío.
It's getting cold now.
B: ¡Sí, por fin! Planeo esquiar los fines de semana.
Yes, finally. I plan to ski on the weekends.
A: El invierno me deprime.
The winter depresses me.
B: Anímate. Piensa que mi inverno es tu verano.
Cheer up. Think that my winter is your summer.

Key Vocabulary

el invierno Noun (m) winter
el frío Noun (m) cold
deprimir Verb to depress
el verano Noun (m) summer
animar Verb to cheer up

Supplementary Vocabulary

la primavera Noun (f) spring
el otoño Noun (m) fall
la estación Noun (f) season
el calor Noun (m) heat
la nieve Noun (f) snow
el sol Noun (m) sun
