Elementary - What a family (B0214)

A:  ¿Arturo es tu hermano?
Is Arturo your brother?
B:  No, es mi primo.
No, he's my cousin.
A:  Entonces Ernesto es tu hermano.
Then Ernesto is your brother.
B:  No, es mi tío.
No, he's my uncle.
A:  Entonces, ¿quién es tu hermano?
So then, who is your brother?
B:  No tengo hermano, tengo hermana.
I don't have a brother, I have a sister.
