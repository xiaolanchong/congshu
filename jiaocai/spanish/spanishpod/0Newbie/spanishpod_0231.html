Newbie - Do you play any instruments? (A0231)

A: ¿Sabes tocar algún instrumento?
Do you know how to play any instruments?
B: Sí, el piano. ¿Y tú?
Yes, the piano. What about you?
A: Yo sé tocar la guitarra.
I know how to play the guitar.

Key Vocabulary

saber Verb to know
tocar Verb to play
el instrumento Noun (m) instrument
el piano Noun (m) piano
la guitarra Noun (f) guitar

Supplementary Vocabulary

la flauta Noun (f) flute
el viento Noun (m) wind
desafinado Adjective out of tune
afinar Verb to tune
la partitura Noun (f) score
la música Noun (f) music
