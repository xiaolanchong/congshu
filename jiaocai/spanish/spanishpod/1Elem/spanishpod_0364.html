Elementary - Need Water (B0364)

A:  Necesito agua.
I need water.
B:  ¿Estás crudo?
Are you hungover?
A:  No, sólo tengo sed.
No, I'm just thirsty.
B:  Entonces crudo.
Then you're hungover.
A:  Si no tomo.
But if I don't drink.
B:  ¡No tomas... consejos!
You don't take advice!
