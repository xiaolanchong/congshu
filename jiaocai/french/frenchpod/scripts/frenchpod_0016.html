Newbie - How is it going? (A0016)

A: Hé! Salut Sébastien!
Hey! Sebastian, hi!
B: Bonjour. Comment ça va?
Good morning. How is it going?
A: Très bien, merci. Et toi, ça va?
Very well, thanks. And you, how is it going?
B: Oui, en pleine forme!
Yes, in good shape!

Key Vocabulary

comment Adverb how
aller Verb to go
très Adverb very
merci Interjection thank you
et Conjunction and

Supplementary Vocabulary

au revoir Phrase goodbye
à bientôt Phrase see you soon
mal Adjective bad
être Verb to be
fatigué Adjective tired
