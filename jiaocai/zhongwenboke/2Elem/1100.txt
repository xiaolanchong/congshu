Elementary - Expired! (B1100)
A  这酸奶真好喝！
  zhè suānnǎi zhēn hǎohē! 
  This yogurt is really delicious!
B  酸奶？我没买酸奶啊。
  suānnǎi? wǒ méi mǎi suānnǎi a. 
  Yogurt? I didn't buy any yogurt.
A  那这是什么？
  nà zhè shì shénme? 
  Then what's this?
B  这是牛奶......
  zhè shì niúnǎi......
  This is milk...
A  过期了！真恶心！我要吐了！
  guòqī le! zhēn ěxīn! wǒ yào tù le! 
  It's past its expiration date! Disgusting! I'm going to throw up!
B  等等，去厕所！
  děng deng, qù cèsuǒ! 
  Wait, go to the bathroom!
