
Intermediate - Airplane Arrival

Intermediate - Airplane Arrival

(C0906)

各位乘客：

gèwèi chéngkè:

All passengers,

本架飞机预计在北京时间16点20分，当地时间3点20分到达机场。

běn jià fēijī yùjì zài Běijīng shíjiān shí liù diǎn èr shí fēn, dāngdì shíjiān sān diǎn èr shí fēn dàodá jīchǎng.

This aircraft is scheduled to arrive at 4:20pm Beijing time.  The local time at our destination upon arrival will be 3:20am.

芝加哥地面温度21.2摄氏度，55.8华氏度。

Zhījiāgē dìmiàn wēndù èr shí yī diǎn èr shèshìdù, wǔ shí wǔ diǎn bā huáshìdù.

The temperature in Chicago is 21.2 degrees Celsius, 55.8 degrees Fahrenheit.

据最新天气预报，当地天气为晴天。

jù zuì xīn tiānqì yùbào, dāngdì tiānqì wéi qíngtiān.

According to the latest local forecast there are clear skies.

感谢您乘坐美国航空公司的航班。

gǎnxiè nín chéngzuò Měiguó hángkōng gōngsī de hángbān.

Thank you for flying with American Airlines.
