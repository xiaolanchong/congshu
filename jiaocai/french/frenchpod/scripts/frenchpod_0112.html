Newbie - I'm going to miss you... (A0112)

A: Tu vas me manquer.
I'm going to miss you.
B: Désolé, je dois partir.
Sorry, I've got to leave.
A: Ne t`en vas pas... je t'aime!
Don't go... I love you!

Key Vocabulary

tu Pronoun you
désolé Interjection sorry
devoir Verb must
partir Verb to leave
je Pronoun I

Supplementary Vocabulary

la déclaration Noun (f) declaration
le coeur brisé Phrase brokenheart
le train Noun (m) train
la gare Noun (f) train station
le quai Noun (m) platform
