Elementary - Thanksgiving (B0482)

A:  ¿Tienes planes para el Día de Gracias ?
Do you have plans for Thanksgiving?
B:  No lo celebro. Soy colombiana.
I don't celebrate it. I'm Colombian.
A:  Cierto. Te invitamos a la casa para cenar pavo con salsa de arándano rojo.
Right. We'd like to invite you over to the house for a dinner of turkey with cranberry sauce.
B:  Muchas gracias. ¿Llevo algo?
Thank you very much. What should I bring?
A:  Tu presencia.
Just yourself.
