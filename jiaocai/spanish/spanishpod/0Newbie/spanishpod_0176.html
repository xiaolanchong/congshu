Newbie - I can't hear you (A0176)

A: Mañana voy a tu casa.
I'm going to your house tomorrow.
B: ¿Perdona? No te oigo.
Sorry? I can't hear you.
A: MAÑANA VOY A TU CASA.
TOMORROW I'M GOING TO YOUR HOUSE.
B: Ahora sí te oigo. Hasta mañana.
I can hear you now. See you tomorrow.

Key Vocabulary

oír Verb to hear
ahora Adverb now
perdonar Verb to excuse
ir Verb to go
la casa Noun (f) house
mañana Adverb tomorrow

Supplementary Vocabulary

escuchar Verb to listen
el oído Noun (m) ear
sordo Adjective deaf
bajo Adjective low
alto Adjective loud
susurrar Verb to whisper
