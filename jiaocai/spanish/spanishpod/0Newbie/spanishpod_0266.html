Newbie - Do you want to share? (A0266)

A: ¡Qué ración tan grande de nachos!
What a big portion of nachos!
B: Sí, es mucho para uno.
Yes, it's a lot for one person.
A: ¿Quieres compartir?
Do you want to share?
B: ¡Sí, compartamos!
Yes, let's share!

Key Vocabulary

compartir Verb to share
la ración Noun (f) portion
grande Adjective large
mucho Adverb a lot
querer Verb to want

Supplementary Vocabulary

generoso Adjective generous
egoísta Adjective selfish
pequeño Adjective small
poco Adverb little
individual Adjective individual
