Newbie - I am hungry (A0101)

A: J`ai faim.
I'm hungry.
B: Moi aussi, j`ai faim.
I'm hungry, too.
A: Et l`aquarium est sale.
And the aquarium is dirty.
B: Au moins on n`a pas soif.
At least we're not thirsty.

Key Vocabulary

la faim Noun (f) hunger
et Conjunction and
sale Adjective dirty
au moins Phrase at least
la soif Noun (f) thirst

Supplementary Vocabulary

manger Verb to eat
boire Verb to drink
se plaindre Verb to complain
nettoyer Verb to clean
le poisson Noun (m) fish
