Intermediate - Internet is Down (C0940)

今天事情太多了。我真想早点儿做完回家。
jīntiān shìqing tài duō le. wǒ zhēn xiǎng zǎo diǎnr zuò wán huíjiā.
I have so much to do today. I really want to finish up early and head home.
啊，上不了网。
à, shàngbuliǎo wǎng.
The internet is down.
有了，有了。
yǒu le, yǒu le.
It's back, it's back.
又没有了。
yòu méiyǒu le.
Nothing again.
上了。
shàng le.
I'm on.
该死的......又上不了网！
gāisǐ de...... yòu shàngbuliǎo wǎng!
Darn... the internet is down again!
真是个破网！
zhēn shì ge pò wǎng!
Stupid internet!
