Newbie - I'm not going (A0172)

A: ¿Vas al museo?
Are you going to the museum?
B: No voy. ¿Tú vas?
I'm not going. Are you going?
A: Sí, voy ahora.
Yes, I'm going now.
B: Hasta pronto.
See you soon.

Key Vocabulary

ir Verb to go
el museo Noun (m) museum
ahora Adverb now
hasta Preposition until
pronto Adverb soon

Supplementary Vocabulary

venir Verb to come
volver Verb to come back
luego Adverb later
la exposición Noun (f) exhibition
el arte Noun (m) art
solo Adjective alone
