Newbie - Do we leave a tip? (A0097)

A: On laisse un pourboire?
Do we leave a tip?
B: Tu es fou.
You're crazy.
A: Mais il est gentil...
But he's nice...
B: Le service est compris.
Service is included.

Key Vocabulary

laisser Verb to leave
le pourboire Noun (m) tip
fou Adjective crazy
gentil Adjective nice
compris Adjective included

Supplementary Vocabulary

le restaurant Noun (m) restaurant
le serveur Noun (m) waiter
la monnaie Noun (f) change
radin Adjective stingy
généreux Adjective generous
l`addition Noun (f) bill
