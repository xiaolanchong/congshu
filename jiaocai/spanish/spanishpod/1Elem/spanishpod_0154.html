Elementary - Bad Driver (B0154)

A: Míralo, no sabe conducir.
Look at him, he doesn't know how to drive.
B: Qué mal conductor, cambia de carril.
What a bad driver. Change lanes.
A: ¿Está borracho o es una mujer?
Is it a drunk or a woman?
B: No empieces.
Don't start!

Key Vocabulary

mal Adjective bad
el conductor Noun (m) driver
conducir Verb to drive
cambiar Verb to change
el carril Noun (m) line
borracho Adjective drunk
empezar Verb to start

Supplementary Vocabulary

bien Adjective good
el chófer Noun (m) driver
temerario Adjective reckless
derecho Adjective right
prudente Adjective careful
