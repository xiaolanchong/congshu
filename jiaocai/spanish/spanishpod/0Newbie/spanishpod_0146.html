Newbie - Where are you going to eat? (A0146)

A: ¿Dónde vas a comer?
Where are you going to eat?
B: Voy a ir al restaurante Casa Paco. ¿Y tú?
I'm going to the Casa Paco restaurant. How about you?
A: No lo sé.
I don't know.
B: Pues vente conmigo.
Well, come with me.
A: Vale.
Alright.

Key Vocabulary

comer Verb to eat
ir Verb to go
el restaurante Noun (m) restaurant
saber Verb to know
venir Verb to come

Supplementary Vocabulary

cenar Verb to have dinner
desayunar Verb to have breakfast
almorzar Verb to have lunch
el hambre Noun (f) hunger
la comida Noun (f) food
la casa Noun (f) home
