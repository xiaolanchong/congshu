Newbie - Let's Party (A0352)

A: ¡Vamos de fiesta!
We're going to party!
B: ¿Qué festejas?
What are you celebrating?
A: Tengo trabajo nuevo.
I have a new job!
B: ¡Felicidades! ¡Tú pagas!
Contratulations! You're paying!

Key Vocabulary

ir Verb to go
la fiesta Noun (f) party
festejar Verb to celebrate
felicidades Interjection congratulations
pagar Verb to pay

Supplementary Vocabulary

invitar Verb to treat
felicitar Verb to congratulate
bailar Verb to dance
beber Verb to drink
el cumpleaños Noun (m) birthday
