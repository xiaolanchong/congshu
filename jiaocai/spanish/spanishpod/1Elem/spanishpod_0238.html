Elementary - Breakfast at the Hotel (B0238)

A:  Mmm, cruasanes...
Mmm, croissants...
B:  Oye, la bollería no es gratis, eh.
Hey, the stuff in the breadbasket isn't free, you know.
A:  Pero qué dices, están aquí en la mesa.
But what are you talking about? They're right here on the table.
B:  Sí, y nos van a cobrar cada uno.
Yes, and they're gong to charge us for each one.
A:  Pues que me cobren. ¡Están ricos!
Then let them charge me. They're delicious!
