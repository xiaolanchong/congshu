Newbie - Going Green (A0013)

A: J`y vais!
I`m leaving!
B: Tu prends la voiture?
Are you taking the car?
A: Non, le métro.
No, the metro.
B: Bravo, c`est plus écologique!
Bravo, it`s more environmentally friendly!

Key Vocabulary

prendre Verb to take
la voiture Noun (f) car
le métro Pronoun metro
être Verb to be
écologique Adjective environmentally friendly

Supplementary Vocabulary

le train Noun (m) train
la pollution Noun (f) pollution
l`air Noun (m) air
l`eau Noun (f) water
l`essence Noun (m) petrol
