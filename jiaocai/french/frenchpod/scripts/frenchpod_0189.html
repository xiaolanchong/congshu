Newbie - What? (A0189)

A: Allô!
Hello!
A: Pardon?
Excuse me?
A: Quoi? Je ne t`entends pas...
What? I can`t hear you...
A: Répète, s`il te plaît.
Please repeat...
A: Attends, je sors.
Wait, I`ll go outside.

Key Vocabulary

le pardon Noun (m) forgiveness (excuse me)
quoi Pronoun what
entendre Verb to hear
répéter Verb to repeat
sortir Verb to go out
allô Interjection hello
attendre Verb to wait

Supplementary Vocabulary

parler Verb to speak
le (téléphone) portable Noun (m) mobile
doucement Adverb softly
fort Adjective loud
comprendre Verb to understand
