Elementary - Who ate my sandwich? (B0331)

A: ¿Quién se comió mi sándwich?
Who ate my sandwich?
B: Yo no fui.
It wasn't me.
A: ¿Qué escondes en la mano?
What are you hiding in your hand?
B: Nada.
Nothing.
A: ¡Enséñame!
Show me!
B: Uy...
Ay...

Key Vocabulary

comer Verb to eat
quién Pronoun who
esconder Verb to hide
la mano Noun (f) hand
nada Pronoun nothing
enseñar Verb to show

Supplementary Vocabulary

culpable Adjective guilty
la comida Noun (f) food
abrir Verb to open
tener Verb to have
importar Verb to matter
