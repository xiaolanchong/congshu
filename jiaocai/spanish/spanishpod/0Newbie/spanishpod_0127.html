Newbie - Flirting (A0127)

A:  ¡Mírala! Está coqueteando.
Look at her! She's flirting.
B:  ¿Por qué?
Why?
A:  Te ve y te sonríe.
She saw you and she smiled.
B:  Perfecto, voy a hablar con ella.
Perfect, I'm going to talk to her.
