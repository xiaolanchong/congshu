Newbie - I miss them (A0131)

A: ¿Qué te pasa?
What's wrong?
B: Extraño a mi familia.
I miss my family.
A: Llámalos.
Call them.
B: Buena idea.
Good idea.

Key Vocabulary

pasar Verb to happen
extrañar Verb to miss
familia Noun (f) family
llamar Verb to call
bueno Adjective good

Supplementary Vocabulary

echar de menos Verb to miss
añorar Verb to miss
recuerdo Noun (m) memory
foto Noun (f) picture
deprimido Adjective depressed
triste Adjective sad
