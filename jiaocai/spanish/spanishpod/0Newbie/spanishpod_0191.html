Newbie - Doesn't work (A0191)

A: No enciende.
It's not turning on.
A: No sirve.
It doesn't work.
B: Con razón, no tiene pilas.
No wonder, it doesn't have batteries.

Key Vocabulary

encender Verb to turn on
servir Verb to work
tener Verb to have
la pila Noun (f) battery
la razón Noun (f) reason

Supplementary Vocabulary

apagar Verb to turn off
gastar Verb to use up
la televisión Noun (f) television
el mando Noun (m) remote control
faltar Verb to lack
