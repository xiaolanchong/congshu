Newbie - Ya vienen los Reyes (A0292)

Ya vienen los Reyes, con el aguinaldo,
The kings are coming, with the gift,
les parece mucho, y le van quitando,
It seems a lot to them, and they give it away,
pampanitos verdes, hojas de limón,
Little vine leaves, lemon leaves,
la Virgen María, madre del Señor.
The Virgin Mary, Mother of the Lord.

Key Vocabulary

venir Verb to come
el rey Noun (m) King
el aguinaldo Noun (m) Christmas bonus
quitar Verb to take off
mucho Adverb a lot

Supplementary Vocabulary

el turrón Noun (m) nougat candy
los Reyes Magos Noun Three Wise Men
poco Adverb little
regalar Verb to give
el regalo Noun (m) gift
