Newbie - Buying a bus ticket (A0181)

A: Un ticket, s`il vous plaît.
One ticket, please.
B: Aller-retour ?
Round-trip?
A: Non, un aller simple.
No, one-way.
B: Ca fait deux euros, Mademoiselle.
That`ll be two euros, Miss.

Key Vocabulary

le ticket Noun (m) ticket
s`il vous plaît Phrase please
l`aller-retour Noun (m) round trip
l`aller simple Noun (m) one way
l`euro Noun (m) euro
Mademoiselle Noun (f) miss

Supplementary Vocabulary

le bus Noun (m) bus
le chauffeur Noun (m) driver
acheter Verb to buy
le passager Noun (m) passenger
le carnet de tickets Noun (m) book of tickets
