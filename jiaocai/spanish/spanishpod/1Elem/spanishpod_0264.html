Elementary - Formal Dinner 3: Would you pass me...? (B0264)

A: Carlos, le importa pasarme un poco de pan, por favor.
Carlos, would you mind passing me a little bread, please?
B: Con mucho gusto, aquí tiene.
With pleasure; here you are.
A: Muchísimas gracias.
Thank you very much.
B: ¿Quiere un poco más de vino?
Would you like a little more wine?
A: Sí, por favor.
Yes, please.

Key Vocabulary

pasar Verb to pass
querer Verb to want
tener Verb to have
poco Adverb little
el pan Noun (m) bread

Supplementary Vocabulary

dar Verb to give
la sal Noun (m) salt
la carta de vinos Phrase wine list
el brócoli Noun (m) broccoli
apetecer Verb to feel like
