Elementary - Crawling (B0401)

A: Ya viste, el bebé está gateando.
Did you see the baby is already crawling.
B: Además lo hace rápido.
On top of that he's does it fast.
A: ¿Crees que se pueda parar?
Do you think he can stop?
B: Cómo crees, acaba de empezar a gatear.
What do you think, he just started to crawl.
A: Bueno, bueno, sólo pregunto.
Okay, okay I'm only asking.

Key Vocabulary

gatear Verb to crawl
el bebé Noun (m) baby
rápido Adjective fast
pararse Verb to stand
empezar Verb to start

Supplementary Vocabulary

andar Verb to walk
caerse Verb to fall
aprender Verb to learn
ponerse de pie Phrase to stand up
despacio Adverb slowly
