Elementary - Stop Eating (B0262)

A:  ¡Deja de comer!
Stop eating!
B:  Casi nunca como papas fritas.
I almost never eat potato chips.
A:  Ayer comiste una bolsa.
Yesterday, you ate a bag.
B:  ¡Eran chicharrones!
Those were pork rinds!
A:  Es lo mismo, la comida chatarra no es saludable. ¡Dame eso!
Same thing, junk food is not healthy. Give me that!
