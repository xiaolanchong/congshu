Elementary - Roller coaster (B0100)

A: On est arrivés au sommet?
Did we get to the top?
B: Ne regarde pas en bas.
Don't look down.
A: Ne t`inquiète pas, je regarde mes chaussures.
Don't worry, I'm looking at my shoes.
B: Allez, plus haut.
Come on, higher!
A: J`ai déjà envie de vomir.
I already feel like throwing up.
B: On y est.
We're there...

Key Vocabulary

arriver Verb to arrive
le sommet Noun (m) top
regarder Verb to look at
en bas Adverb down
la chaussure Noun (f) shoe
haut Adjective high
l`envie Noun (f) wish

Supplementary Vocabulary

les montagnes russes Noun (f) roller coaster
le vertige Noun (f) fear of heights
la fête foraine Noun (f) fun fair
la barbe à papa Noun (f) cotton candy
le frisson Noun (m) shudder
