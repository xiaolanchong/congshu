2. Reflexive pronoun

se / sebi / seboj — used for all persons; no nominative form.

Vidim se. (I see myself.)
Moral si je pogledati v sebi. (He should look into himself.)

4. Demonstrative & interrogative pronouns

Demonstrative
  • ta (this)
  • tisti / on (that)
Interrogative
  • kdo (who)
  • kaj (what)
  • kateri (which)
  • čigav (whose)

5. Indefinite & negative pronouns (common)

  • nekdo (someone), nekaj (something)
  • kdorkoli (anyone), kar koli (anything)
  • nihče (no one), nič (nothing)
