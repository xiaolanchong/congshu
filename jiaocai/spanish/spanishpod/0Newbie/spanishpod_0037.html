Newbie - The baby is sleeping (A0037)

A: ¡Shhh! No hagas ruido.
Shhh! Don't make noise!
B: ¿Por qué?
Why?
A: El bebé está dormido.
The baby is sleeping.
B: No te preocupes.
Don't worry.

Key Vocabulary

hacer Verb to make
el ruido Noun (m) noise
el bebé Noun (m) baby
estar Verb to be
dormir Verb to sleep
preocupar Verb to worry

Supplementary Vocabulary

llorar Verb to cry
gatear Verb to crawl
balbucear Verb to babble
el biberón Noun (m) baby bottle
