Elementary - Which one is your car? (B0179)

A:  Date prisa que es tarde.
Hurry up, it's late.
B:  ¿Cuál es tu carro?
Which is your car?
A:  El que está estacionado al fondo.
The one that's parked in the back.
B:  ¿El azul?
The blue one?
A:  No, el negro.
No, the black one.
