Newbie - My name is... (A0082)

A: Hola, ¿cómo te llamas?
Hello, what's your name?
B: Me llamo Antonia, ¿y tú?
I'm called Antonia, and you?
A: Yo soy Roberto.
I'm Roberto.
B: Encantada.
Charmed.
A: Mucho gusto.
It's a pleasure.

Key Vocabulary

llamarse Verb to be called
ser Verb to be
encantado Adjective charmed
mucho Adjective a lot of
gusto Noun (m) pleasure

Supplementary Vocabulary

presentar Verb to introduce
saludar Verb to greet
apellido Noun (m) last name
placer Noun (m) pleasure
conocer Verb to meet
