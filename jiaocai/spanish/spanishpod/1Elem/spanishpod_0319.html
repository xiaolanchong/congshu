Elementary - She hates potatoes (B0319)

A:  ¿Quieres unas papas fritas?
Do you want fried potatoes?
B:  No le gustan las papas.
She doesn't like potatoes.
A:  ¡Qué! Nadie odia las papas.
What! No one hates potatoes.
B:  Ella sí.
She does.
A:  Papas a la francesa, papa al horno, ¿todas?
French fries, baked potatoes, everything?
B:  Sí.
Yes.
