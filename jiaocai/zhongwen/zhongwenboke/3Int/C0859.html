Intermediate - Regional Accents (C0859)

你打算什么时候生小孩？
nǐ dǎsuàn shénme shíhou shēng xiǎohái?
When do you plan to have a child?
来了多少人？
lái le duōshao rén?
How many people came?
我和哥哥都喜欢画画。
wǒ hé gēge dōu xǐhuan huàhuà.
My brother and I like to paint.

Key Vocabulary

打算 dǎsuàn to plan
什么时候 shénme shíhou when
生小孩 shēng xiǎohái to give birth
来 lái to come
多少 duōshao how many
和 hé and
哥哥 gēge older brother
都 dōu all
喜欢 xǐhuan to like
画画 huàhuà to paint; to draw
