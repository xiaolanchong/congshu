Newbie - Wait for me (A0601)

A:  Espérame. Sin mí no vas.
Wait for me. You are not leaving without me.
B:  Siempre lo mismo. Eres muy impuntual.
It's always the same thing. You are so unpunctual.
A:  No me dejes. Dame dos minutos.
Don't leave me. Give me two minutes.
B:  Te doy un minuto. Si no bajas, me voy.
I will give you one minute. If you are not downstairs, I will leave.
