Newbie - How much is it? (A0018)

A: Vous avez des champignons, s`il vous plait?
Do you have any mushrooms, please?
B: Oui, Madame!
Yes Ma`am.
A: Combien ça coûte?
How much does it cost?
B: Pour vous, 5 euros!
For you, 5 euros!

Key Vocabulary

s`il vous plait Phrase please (formal)
combien Adverb how much
coûter Verb to cost
pour Preposition for
l`euro Noun (m) euro

Supplementary Vocabulary

le marché Noun (m) market
le prix Noun (m) price
acheter Verb to buy
payer Verb to pay
Monsieur Noun (m) Sir
