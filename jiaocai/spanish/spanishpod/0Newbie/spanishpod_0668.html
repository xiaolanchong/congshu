Newbie - Meet the family (A0668)

A: Te presento a mi primo y a mi tío.
Let me introduce you to my cousin and my uncle.
B: Mucho gusto.
Nice to meet you.
A: Ella es mi sobrina y allá está mi hermano.
This is my niece and over there is my brother.
B: ¿Y ella?
And her?
A: Mi esposa.
My wife.

Key Vocabulary

presentar to introduce
el primo cousin
la sobrina niece
el hermano brother
el tío uncle

Supplementary Vocabulary

el abuelo grandfather
la cuñada sister-in-law
la suegra mother-in-law
el compadre godfather to one's child
la madrina godmother
