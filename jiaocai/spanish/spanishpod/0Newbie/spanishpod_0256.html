Newbie - How much is it? (A0256)

A: ¿Cuánto cuesta?
How much does it cost?
B: Cien euros.
One hundred euros.
A: ¡Qué caro!
How expensive!
B: Pues venga en rebajas.
Then come back during the sales.

Key Vocabulary

caro Adjective expensive
cuánto Pronoun how much
costar Verb to cost
venir Verb to come
la rebaja Noun (f) sale

Supplementary Vocabulary

barato Adjective cheap
valer Verb to cost
la oferta Noun (f) offer
el descuento Noun (m) discount
hacer descuento Phrase to give a discount
la ganga Noun (f) bargain
