Elementary - Beauty Secrets (B0028)

A: C`est horrible! J`ai la peau sèche.
That`s terrible! My skin is dry.
B: Tu mets de la crème?
Do you use cream?
A: Ca marche vraiment, ça?
Does that really work?
B: Mais oui. Essaie la mienne!
Of course, it does. Try mine!
A: Salut les garçons! De quoi vous parlez?
Hey, boys! What are you talking about?
B: De voitures!
About cars!
A: De foot!
About football!

Key Vocabulary

la peau Noun (f) skin
sec Adjective dry
la crème Noun (f) cream
marcher Verb to work
essayer Verb try
quoi Pronoun what
parler Verb to talk
