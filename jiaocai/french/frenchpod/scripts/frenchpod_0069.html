Newbie - Is someone sitting here? (A0069)

A: Excusez-moi. Il y a quelqu'un?
Excuse me. Is someone sitting here?
B: Non, je vous en prie.
No, please go ahead.
A: Merci. C'est gentil.
Thank you. It's nice of you.
B: De rien.
You're welcome.
