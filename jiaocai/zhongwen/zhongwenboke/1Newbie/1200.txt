Newbie - A Family of Teachers (A1205)
A  李老师，我爸爸也是老师。
  Lǐ lǎoshī, wǒ bàba yě shì lǎoshī. 
  Mr. Li, my dad is a teacher, too!
B  是吗？你妈妈呢？
  shì ma? nǐ māma ne? 
  Really? What about your mom?
A  我妈妈也是老师。
  wǒ māma yě shì lǎoshī. 
  My mom is also a teacher.
B  哇，你家都是老师！
  wā, nǐ jiā dōu shì lǎoshī! 
  Whoa, everyone in your family is a teacher!

老师  lǎoshī  teacher
也  yě  also
