Newbie - Pass the ball (A0239)

A: Passe la balle.
Pass the ball.
A: Plus vite, plus vite. Ils arrivent!
Faster, faster. They`re coming!
B: Où sont- ils?
Where are they?
A: Là, derrière toi!
There, behind you!

Key Vocabulary

Là there
derrière behind
arriver to arrive
Passer to pass
vite fast
