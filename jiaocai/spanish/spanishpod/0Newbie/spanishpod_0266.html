Newbie - Do you want to share? (A0266)

A:  ¡Qué ración tan grande de nachos!
What a big portion of nachos!
B:  Sí, es mucho para uno.
Yes, it's a lot for one person.
A:  ¿Quieres compartir?
Do you want to share?
B:  ¡Sí, compartamos!
Yes, let's share!
