Newbie - Watch out! (A0234)

A: Cuidado.
Careful.
B: ¿Qué dices?
What are you saying?
A: Que tengas cuidado.
That you should be careful.
B: Ok, gracias.
Ok, thanks.

Key Vocabulary

el cuidado Noun (m) caution
qué Pronoun what
decir Verb to say
tener cuidado Phrase to be careful
gracias Interjection thanks

Supplementary Vocabulary

peligroso Adjective dangerous
prudente Adjective cautious
avisar Verb to warn
advertir Verb to warn
cruzar Verb to cross
