Intermediate - Dog Personalities

(C0475)

我想养狗。你能给我推荐一些好的品种吗？

wǒ xiǎng yǎng gǒu 。nǐ néng gěi wǒ tuījiàn yīxiē hǎo de pǐnzhǒng ma ？

I want to get a dog.  Could you recommend some good breeds?

那要看你喜欢什么样的。有些人喜欢活泼一点的狗，有些人却喜欢温顺一点的。

nà yào kàn nǐ xǐhuan shénmeyàng de 。yǒuxiē rén xǐhuan huópo yīdiǎn de gǒu ，yǒuxiē rén què xǐhuan wēnshùn yīdiǎn de 。

That depends on what you like.  Some people like dogs that are a little more lively, others like a more docile dog.

越乖越好。我听说有些小狗很吵，大狗反而安静。

yuè guāi yuè hǎo 。wǒ tīngshuō yǒuxiē xiǎo gǒu hěn chǎo ，dà gǒu fǎnér ānjìng 。

The more obedient, the better.  I’ve heard that some small dogs are very noisy; that big dogs, on the other hand, are quiet.

也不完全是。其实最重要的是训练。

yě bù wánquán shì 。qíshí zuì zhòngyào de shì xùnliàn 。

That’s not altogether true.  Actually, the most important thing is training.

那不是要花很多心思吗？

nà bù shì yào huā hěn duō xīnsi ma ？

Do you have to put a lot of thought into it?

当然咯。养狗就像养孩子，主人必须专心爱它、培养它。这是主人的责任。

dāngrán lo 。yǎng gǒu jiùxiàng yǎng háizi ，zhǔrén bìxū zhuānxīn ài tā ，péiyǎng tā 。zhè shì zhǔrén de zérèn 。

Of course.  Keeping a dog is like raising a child—the owner must concentrate on showing it love, and training it.  This is a dog owner's responsibility.

啊？比我想象的麻烦多了。我只想有条狗陪我，跟我玩。

á ？bǐ wǒ xiǎngxiàng de máfan duō le 。wǒ zhǐ xiǎng yǒu tiáo gǒu péi wǒ ，gēn wǒ wán 。

Huh?  It’s a lot more trouble than I had imagined.  I was only thinking about having a dog to keep me company and play with me.

不行，你这种想法太自私了。你根本没有做好养狗的准备。

bùxíng ，nǐ zhèzhǒng xiǎngfǎ tài zìsī le 。nǐ gēnběn méiyǒu zuò hǎo yǎng gǒu de zhǔnbèi 。

No way.  Your viewpoint is too selfish.  You are not at all ready to take care of a dog.

是啊。我想我还是先养好自己吧。

shì a 。wǒ xiǎng wǒ háishì xiān yǎng hǎo zìjǐ ba 。

Yeah.  I think I still need to first take good care of myself.
