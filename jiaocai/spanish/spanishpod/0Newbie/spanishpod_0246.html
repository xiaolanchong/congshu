Newbie - Trying on clothes (A0246)

A: Me queda pequeña.
It's small on me.
B: ¿Qué talla es?
What size is it?
A: La talla S.
It's a small.
B: Pues pruébate la M, burro.
Then try on a medium.

Key Vocabulary

quedar Verb to fit
pequeño Adjective small
la talla Noun (f) size
probar Verb to try on
pues Adverb then

Supplementary Vocabulary

mediano Adjective medium
grande Adjective large
bien Adverb ok
mal Adverb bad
justo Adjective tight
