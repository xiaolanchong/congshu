Newbie - What's going on? (A0032)

A: Qu`est-ce qui se passe?
What`s going on?
B: Il y a le président!
It`s the president!
A: Je ne vois pas.
I can`t see.
B: Ah! C`est dommage, il est avec sa femme.
Oh! That`s too bad, he`s with his wife.

Key Vocabulary

se passer Verb to happen
il y a Phrase there is
le président Noun (m) president
voir Verb to see
c`est dommage Phrase too bad
la femme Noun (f) wife

Supplementary Vocabulary

la foule Noun (f) crowd
l`homme politique Noun (m) politician
le mari Noun (m) husband
regarder Verb to look at
le mannequin Noun (f) model
