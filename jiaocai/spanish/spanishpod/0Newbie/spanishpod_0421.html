Newbie - Don't jump! (A0421)

A:  Pero, ¿qué hace ahí arriba?
But what is he doing up there?
B:  ¿Está loco?
Is he crazy?
A:  Ay no, se quiere tirar de la fuente.
Oh no, he wants to jump from the fountain.
B:  No, no saltes, es peligroso.
No, don't jump, it's dangerous.
