Newbie - Don't jump! (A0421)

A: Pero, ¿qué hace ahí arriba?
But what is he doing up there?
B: ¿Está loco?
Is he crazy?
A: Ay no, se quiere tirar de la fuente.
Oh no, he wants to jump from the fountain.
B: No, no saltes, es peligroso.
No, don't jump, it's dangerous.

Key Vocabulary

arriba Adverb up
loco Adjective crazy
la fuente Noun (f) fountain
querer Verb to want
saltar Verb to jump

Supplementary Vocabulary

beber Verb to drink
el agua Noun (f) water
potable Adjective drinkable
seguro Adjective safe
alto Adverb high
locura Noun (f) insanity
