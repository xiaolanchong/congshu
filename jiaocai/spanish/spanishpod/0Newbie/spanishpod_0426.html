Newbie - Shave it! (A0426)

A:  ¿Y esa barba?
What's the deal with the beard?
B:  ¿Te gusta?
Do you like it?
A:  No, ¡rasúrate!
No, shave it!
B:  No tengo rastrillo.
I don't have a razor.
A:  ¡Pues compra uno!
Well, buy one!
