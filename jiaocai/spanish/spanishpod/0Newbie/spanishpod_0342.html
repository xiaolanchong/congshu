Newbie - Can't find it (A0342)

A: ¡No lo encuentro!
I can't find it!
B: Búscalo en la cocina.
Look for it in the kitchen.
A: ¡Tampoco está allí!
It's not there either!
B: Entonces lo perdiste.
Then you lost it.
A: Noooooo.
Noooooo.

Key Vocabulary

encontrar Verb to find
buscar Verb to search for
tampoco Adverb neither
perder Verb to lose
la cocina Noun (f) kitchen

Supplementary Vocabulary

el paraguas Noun (m) umbrella
la cartera Noun (f) wallet
la bolsa Noun (f) bag
mirar Verb to look at
el móvil Noun (m) cell phone
el carro Noun (m) car
