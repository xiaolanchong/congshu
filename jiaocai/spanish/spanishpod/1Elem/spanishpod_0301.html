Elementary - Three Wise Men (B0301)

A:  ¿Quiénes son los Reyes Magos?
Who are the three wise men?
B:  Son tres: Melchor, Gaspar y Baltasar.
There are three of them: Melchior, Caspar, and Balthasar.
A:  ¿Melchor es el de la barba blanca?
Is Melchior the one with the white beard?
B:  Sí. Gaspar es el de la barba negra y Baltasar el de raza negra.
Yes. Caspar is the one with the black beard, and Balthasar is racially black.
A:  ¿Y van en camello?
And they travel by camel?
B:  Claro.
Of course.
