Newbie - Check, please. (A0312)

A: ¿Algo más?
Anything else?
B: No, gracias.
No, thanks.
A: ¿Todo bien?
Is everything alright?
B: Sí, me trae la cuenta, por favor.
Yes, bring me the bill, please.
A: Enseguida.
Right away.

Key Vocabulary

algo Pronoun something
bien Adverb well
traer Verb to bring
la cuenta Noun (f) bill
enseguida Adverb right away

Supplementary Vocabulary

pagar Verb to pay
dulce Adjective sweet
nada Pronoun nothing
atender Verb to attend
el camarero Noun (m) waiter
