Newbie - It's fun! Try it! (A0127)

A: Tu es vraiment un enfant.
You're such a child.
B: Mais c`est drôle. Essaie.
But it's fun. Try it.
A: Bon d`accord. C`est vrai. Tu as raison. C`est vraiment drôle!
It's true. You're right. It's really fun!

Key Vocabulary

l`enfant Noun (m) child
essayer Verb to try
la raison Noun (f) reason
vraiment Adverb really
drôle Adjective funny

Supplementary Vocabulary

jouer Verb to play
l`automne Noun (m) autumn
la feuille Noun (f) leaf
sauter Verb to jump
enfantin Adjective child-like
