Newbie - Turn down the music (A0212)

A: Tu peux baisser le son s`il te plaît?
Can you turn down the music please?
B: Quoi? Parle plus fort, je n`entends rien.
What? Speak louder, I cannot hear anything.
A: Moins fort la musique !
Drop the music!
B: Attends, je vais diminuer le volume... Je ne comprends rien.
Wait, I am going to lower the volume, I don`t understand anything.

Key Vocabulary

baisser Verb to turn down
le son Noun (m) sound
fort Adjective loud
rien Adverb nothing
le volume Noun (m) volume
entendre Verb to hear
comprendre Verb to understand
plus Adverb more
moins Adverb less

Supplementary Vocabulary
