Elementary - Imprudencia (B0547)

A:  ¡Un río! Me voy a dar un chapuzón.
A river! I'm going in for a dip.
B:  ¡Es zona prohibida!
This area's off limits!
A:  ¡Tonterías! No pasa nada.
That's dumb. Nothing bad will happen.
B:  No seas imprudente.
Don't do anything foolish.
A:  ¡Mira y calla!
Shut up and watch!
B:  ¡Allá tú con los cocodrilos!
You're on your own with the crocodiles!
A:  ¡Auxilioooooooo!
Heeeeeeeeeeeeeeelllllp!
