Intermediate - Using Excel

(C1965)

A:

小赵，你那个表格做好了吗？

Xiǎo Zhào, nǐ nà ge biǎogé zuò hǎo le ma?

Xiao Zhao, have you finished that table?

B:

还没有。

hái méiyǒu.

Not yet.

A:

怎么回事？两天了还没做完？

zěnmehuíshì? liǎng tiān le hái méi zuò wán?

How can that be? You still haven't finished it after two days?

B:

数据很多，有很多东西要计算。

shùjù hěn duō, yǒu hěn duō dōngxi yào jìsuàn.

There's a lot of data and a lot of calculations to do.

A:

Excel会自己计算。

Excel huì zìjǐ jìsuàn.

Excel can do the calculations itself.

B:

怎么算？

zěnme suàn?

How?

A:

用公式！你以前没学过？

yòng gōngshì! nǐ yǐqián méi xué guo?

Use a formula! You haven't learned this before?

B:

嗯。

ng4.

No.

A:

你看，在这里输入公式，它就算出来了。

nǐ kàn, zài zhèlǐ shūrù gōngshì, tā jiù suàn chūlái le.

Look. Enter the formula here and it'll just calculate it.

B:

那下面的呢？

nà xiàmian de ne?

What about the stuff below?

A:

这样往下一拉，就都算出来了。

zhèyàng wǎng xià yī lā, jiù dōu suàn chūlái le.

Pull it down like this and it'll calculate it.

B:

这么简单！

zhème jiǎndān!

It's so simple!

A:

是啊，很简单。快点儿做吧！

shì a, hěn jiǎndān. kuài diǎnr zuò ba!

Yep, it's very simple. Finish it up quick!
