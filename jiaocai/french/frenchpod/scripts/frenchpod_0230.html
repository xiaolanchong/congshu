Newbie - How well do you know this girl? (A0230)

A: Tu connais bien cette fille?
Do you know that girl very well?
B: Pas trop, mais je la trouve canon! Pourquoi?
Not too well, but I think she`s gorgeous! Why?
A: C`est ma prof de massage...
That`s my massage teacher...
B: Encore mieux! Tu as son numéro?
Even better! Do you have her number?

Key Vocabulary

connaître Verb to know
la fille Noun (f) girl
trouver Verb to find
canon Adjective gorgeous
pourquoi Adverb why
la prof Noun (m) teacher
le massage Noun (f) massage
encore mieux Phrase even better
le numéro Noun (m) number

Supplementary Vocabulary

le professeur Noun (m) teacher
joli Adjective pretty
l`ami Noun (m) friend
inviter Verb to invite
appeler Verb to call
oser Verb to dare
audacieux Adjective audacious
