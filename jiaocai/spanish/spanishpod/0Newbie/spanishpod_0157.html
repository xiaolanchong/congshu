Newbie - What floor? (A0157)

A: Hola, buenos días.
Hello, good morning.
B: Buenos días.
Good morning.
A: ¿A qué piso va?
What floor?
B: Al quinto.
Fifth.

Key Vocabulary

hola Interjection hello
buenos días Interjection good morning
el piso Noun (m) floor
ir Verb to go
quinto Adjective fifth

Supplementary Vocabulary

el ascensor Noun (m) elevator
la escalera Noun (f) stairs
subir andando Verb to take the stairs up
subir Verb to go up
bajar Verb to go down
vivir Verb to live
