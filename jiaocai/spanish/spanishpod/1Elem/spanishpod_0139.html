Elementary - What would you like to drink? (B0139)

A: ¡Hola!
Hi!
B: ¡Hola! Pasa, ¿qué quieres tomar?
Hi! Come in! What would you like to drink?
A: ¿Qué tienes?
What do you have?
B: Refresco, cerveza, vino y limonada.
Soda, beer, wine, and lemonade.
A: Limonada, por favor.
Lemonade, please.

Key Vocabulary

tomar Verb to drink
querer Verb to want
tener Verb to have
pasar Verb to come in
el refresco Noun (m) soda
la cerveza Noun (f) beer
el vino Noun (m) wine
la limonada Noun (f) lemonade

Supplementary Vocabulary

beber Verb to drink
el hielo Noun (m) ice
la bebida Noun (f) drink
frío Adjective cold
la sed Noun thirst
