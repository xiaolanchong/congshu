Newbie - Making the Bridge (A0136)

A: Tu travailles lundi?
Do you work Monday?
B: Non, mardi est un jour férié.
No, Tuesday is a holiday.
A: Tu fait le pont, alors ?
So you're making the bridge?
B: Bien sûr.
Of course.

Key Vocabulary

travailler Verb to work
le lundi Adverb on Monday
le mardi Adverb Tuesday
le jour férié Noun (m) bank holiday
faire le pont Phrase take the day between the holiday and the weekend off

Supplementary Vocabulary

le mercredi Noun (m) Wednesday
le jeudi Noun (m) Thursday
le vendredi Noun (m) Friday
le samedi Noun (m) Saturday
le dimanche Noun (m) Sunday
la semaine Noun (f) week
le weekend Noun (m) week-end
