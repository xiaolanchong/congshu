Newbie - Movie Tickets (A0207)

A: Tengo boletos para el cine.
I have tickets for the movies.
B: ¿Puedo ir contigo?
Can I go with you?
A: No.
No.

Key Vocabulary

el boleto Noun (m) ticket
el cine Noun (m) cinema
tener Verb to have
ir Verb to go
poder Verb can
contigo Pronoun with you

Supplementary Vocabulary

comprar Verb to buy
acompañar Verb to accompany
la entrada Noun (f) ticket
venir Verb to come
conmigo Pronoun with me
las palomitas Noun (f) popcorn
