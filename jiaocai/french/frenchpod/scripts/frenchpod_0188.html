Elementary - Skiing Lesson (B0188)

A: Attention, attention, j`arrive, écartez-vous!
Watch out, watch out, I`m coming, get out of the way!
B: Mais freine, freine.
But slow down, slow down.
A: Je ne sais pas comment!
I don`t know how!
B: Allez, debout.
Come on, stand up!
A: Je crois que je me suis fait mal en tombant.
I think I hurt myself when I fell.
B: On verra ça en bas des pistes. Allez, sur tes skis.
We`ll check when we`re at the bottom of the slope. Come on, get back on your skis.

Key Vocabulary

attention Interjection watch out
s`écarter Verb move
freiner Verb slow down
comment Adverb how
aller Verb come on
