Newbie - Scary Noise (A0020)

A: Tu entends ce bruit?
Can you hear this noise?
B: Non... Maintenant oui!
No... Now yes!
A: J`ai peur!
I`m scared!
B: Moi aussi!
Me too!

Key Vocabulary

entendre Verb to hear
le bruit Noun (m) noise
avoir Verb to have
la peur Noun (f) fear
aussi Adverb too

Supplementary Vocabulary

le courage Noun (m) courage
rassurer Verb to reassure
le silence Noun (m) silence
le loup Noun (m) wolf
le camping Noun (m) camping
