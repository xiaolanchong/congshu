Elementary - Bossy (B0109)

A: Cambia de canal, quiero ver la telenovela.
Change the channel, I want to watch the soap opera.
B: No quiero, estoy viendo el partido.
I don't want to, I'm watching the game.
A: Venga, cámbialo.
Come on, change it.
B: Eres una mandona.
You're bossy.
A: No lo soy.
No, I'm not.
B: Sí que lo eres.
Yes, you are.

Key Vocabulary

canal Noun (m) channel
telenovela Noun (f) soap opera
partido Noun (m) game
mandona Adjective bossy
cambiar Verb to change
venga Interjection come on
ser Verb to be
querer Verb to want

Supplementary Vocabulary

serio Adjective reliable
amable Adjective nice
agradable Adjective pleasant
obediente Adjective obedient
mandar Verb to order
obedecer Verb to obey
