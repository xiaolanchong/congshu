Newbie - Bottle of Water (A0066)

A: ¿Qué le doy?
What can I get you?
B: Me da una botella de agua, por favor.
Give me a bottle of water, please.
A: ¿Agua con gas o sin gas?
Carbonated water or non-carbonated?
B: Sin gas, porque me da gas.
Non-carbonated, because it gives me gas.
A: En seguida se la doy.
I'll get it for you right away.
B: Gracias.
Thank you.

Key Vocabulary

la botella Noun (f) bottle
el agua Noun (f) water
dar Verb to give
el gas Noun (m) gas
sin Preposition without
en seguida Adverb right away

Supplementary Vocabulary

beber Verb to drink
la bebida Noun (f) beverage
tener sed Verb to be thirsty
el refresco Noun (m) soda
el zumo Noun (m) juice
de nada Interjection you are welcome
