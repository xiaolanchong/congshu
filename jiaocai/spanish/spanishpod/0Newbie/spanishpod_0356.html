Newbie - Can you pick them up? (A0356)

A: Voy a la farmacia.
I'm going to the pharmacy.
B: ¿Pasas por los niños?
Are you going to pick up the kids?
A: No están de camino.
They're not on the way.
B: ¡Es tu turno!
It's your turn!
A: Vale, vale.
Okay, okay!

Key Vocabulary

ir Verb to go
pasar Verb to come
de camino Phrase on the way
el turno Noun (m) turn
vale Interjection okay

Supplementary Vocabulary

de acuerdo Interjection ok
el gimnasio Noun (m) gym
el supermercado Noun (m) supermarket
la abuela Noun (f) grandma
desviarse Verb to go off course
